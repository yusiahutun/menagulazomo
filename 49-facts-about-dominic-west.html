Early Life

Dominic West was born on October 15, 1969, in Sheffield, England. He grew up in a musical family with his mother being a concert pianist and his father a theater critic.

Education

West attended Eton College, a prestigious boarding school in England. He then went on to study at Trinity College, Dublin, where he graduated with an honors degree in English Literature.

West’s acting career started on stage, where he performed in various theater productions including Shakespearean plays. He gained critical acclaim for his performances and quickly transitioned to film and television.
