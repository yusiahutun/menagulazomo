MANAGEMENT NEWS: Doug Banker Joins McGhee Entertainment New Artist Management Firm Formed Wo

Publish date: 2024-02-07

Doug Banker, Ted Nugent's longtime manager has joined Doc McGhee's McGhee Entertainment in Los Angeles. Banker, who brings Nugent and pop artist Ta Ta with him, will relocate to LA.

In other McGhee news, Hootie & the Blowfish have signed with the management company.


New Artist Management Firm Formed


Manager Gary Borman has teamed with MCS Group, a Nashville-based entertainment marketing and research firm to establish a new artist management and strategic marketing firm, Ensight. Borman's current management company, Gary Borman Management, will remain a separate entity.


Woolley Forms Tour de Force


Timm Woolley has formed Tour de Force, which will specialize in music tour finance and management. Initial clients include Bon Jovi and Nine Inch Nails. Woolley was formerly with Concerts West.
