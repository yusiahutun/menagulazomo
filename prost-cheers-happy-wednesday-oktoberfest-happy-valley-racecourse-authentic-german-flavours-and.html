Prost (cheers) to Happy Wednesday Oktoberfest at Happy Valley Racecourse with authentic German flavo

Publish date: 2024-05-12

[The content of this article has been produced by our advertising partner.]

As the thrill of the 2023 racing season kicked off at the Happy Valley Racecourse, the calendar has turned its page to October, ushering in the most jovial of all festivals – the Happy Wednesday Oktoberfest. Starting from October 4, this vibrant month-long celebration is set to return in full force at the Happy Valley Racecourse, drawing in party enthusiasts and thrill-seekers alike.

Hong Kong’s beloved sport and lifestyle destination, the Happy Valley Racecourse has been buzzing with excitement. Following closely on the heels of the “Greatest Show in Town,” the city is gearing up for its biggest outdoor Oktoberfest. The event promises to be a spectacle of fun and frolic, making it the must-go extravaganza in the city’s social calendar. So, brace yourself for a rollercoaster of good times at this spectacular festivity.
 
Throughout the month, the Happy Wednesday Oktoberfest transforms Happy Valley Racecourse into a lively hub of German festivities, hosting one of the city’s largest outdoor Oktoberfest celebrations. Revellers and racing lovers are whisked away to the vibrant heart of Bavaria. It promises an authentic German carnival experience vibrating with electric energy in the bustling district of Happy Valley. This unique lifestyle hotspot is where the exhilaration of sports meets the pulsating vibe of a party, creating an irresistible blend of fun and excitement. Nestled amidst sprawling green expanses and set against the city’s glittering nighttime skyline, the Happy Valley Racecourse promises an unforgettable experience. What’s more, with public transport conveniently accessible, this haven of entertainment is just a ride away. So, get ready to immerse yourself in a world where fun knows no bounds.

The Oktoberfest originated from a grand horse race held in Munich in 1810 to celebrate a royal wedding. This event quickly transformed into a large-scale carnival, drawing in festive crowds from far and wide who indulged in the delights of beer and food. The festive spirit of this celebration left an indelible mark on Munich, inspiring the city to carry on this tradition. Today this much-loved festival has also found a home in Hong Kong’s Happy Valley.

In a grand spectacle befitting the return of Happy Wednesday Oktoberfest at the Happy Valley Racecourse, a ceremonial inauguration is set to usher in the month-long jubilations. A distinguished delegate from the German Consulate, accompanied by HKJC’s CEO, Mr. Winfried Engelbrecht-Bresges, will honour the age-old Oktoberfest custom of barrel tapping. This will symbolise the onset of a carnival brimming with delight and festivity.

For the entire Happy Wednesday Oktoberfest, the racecourse will come alive with a series of exhilarating experiences, each one outdoing the previous. We extend an invitation to you to join in the joyous festivities.

The vibrant carnival-like atmosphere is set to escalate with the harmonious tunes of an authentic German band. A plethora of attractions will immerse all revellers in amazing festive vibe: lively band performances that will have you tapping your feet in rhythm, interactive stage games that will keep you engaged, and enchanting traditional folk-dance performances that will leave you spellbound. The festivities promise to sweep you and your companions into the authentic Bavarian atmosphere. Raise a toast to your champion jockeys and racehorses with a selection of fine German brews. The unbridled energy and merriment of Happy Wednesday Oktoberfest are sure to leave all attendees with joyful memories.  

Prepare your palate for a gourmet journey to Bavaria, right in the heart of town. Traditional Bavarian delights galore await, including succulent sausages, crackling pork knuckle and oven-fresh pretzels, satisfying everyone’s taste buds. The much-loved food truck, back by popular demand after a triumphant season debut, completes this eclectic and joyous culinary adventure.

The jubilant vibe of the Beer Garden will be alive every Wednesday throughout the month. Revellers can immerse themselves in the rhythmic melodies of renowned local bands while indulging in authentic German cuisine. This weekly extravaganza enhances the Oktoberfest celebrations with an additional sprinkle of amusement and entertainment.
 
The gates of Happy Wednesday swing wide open for all, free of charge, until December.
 
This October, the Happy Valley Racecourse invites you to dive into an unforgettable Oktoberfest experience that marries the adrenaline rush of horse racing with the vibrant essence of Germany’s favourite festival. Raise your glasses and say Prost! (Cheers!)
