Hard Summer Organizers Announce A New Venue For 2024

Publish date: 2024-06-12

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — HARD Events announced shared some of their plans for the 2024 return of the HARD Summer Music Festival, including the fest’s dates and a brand new venue.

Now one of the largest EDM events on the West Coast, HARD Summer is a multi-genre HARD Summer will take place at Inglewood’s Hollywood Park, located adjacent to SoFi Stadium, on August 3 and 4, 2024.

The festival’s new venue will provide HARD Summer with room to stretch out across the 300-acre mixed-use development with space for dancing, exploration, interactive features, and activations from partners.

“We are thrilled to partner with HARD Events to bring another festival featuring diverse programming to Inglewood,” said Adolfo Romero, VP, Programming and Booking for SoFi Stadium and Hollywood Park. “This partnership not only amplifies Hollywood Park’s influence in the festival space but also solidifies our dedication to curating global entertainment that authentically mirrors our vibrant local community.”

For 2024, the festival will feature multiple stages of music, including the Marquee Main stage and the Pink Stage, which will host house and techno. The HARDER Stage will host an array of cross-genre artists while the Green Stage will be the fest’s home for bass and dubstep. The festival’ Purple stage will feature both club-minded DJ sets along with live performances.

The full lineup for 2024 has yet to be announced but the artist roster from 2023 included Kaskade, John Summit, Skrillex, Four Tet, Kid Cudi, 21 Savage, Dillon Francis, Ludacris, Black Coffee, Oliver Tree, DIESEL, and more.
