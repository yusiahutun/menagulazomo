Angela Fell In Love With The Theatre At Her First Glance

When Angela was 2, her father, Michael Goethals, abandoned his family, leaving the actress and her sister Sara in her mother's care.

Her mother, Rosalind, worked as an assistant stage manager and held a brief tenure for the Shakespeare Company. She used to take both of her daughters to her work. Angela fell in love with theatre at first sight, and it was then that she decided to become a fully-fledged actress.

Angela Goethals is famous for her role in Home Alone. SOURCE: IMDb

Angela stepped into the film industry in her early adolescence. Her family friend and actress Jeanie Hackett introduced the stunning actress to the acting world. They attended several auditions where Angela released her natural dramatic ability, which astounded everyone.

In 1988, she first debuted in the movie Rocket Gibraltar, directed by Daniel Petrie. She also appeared in the 1990 megahit movie "Home Alone" with Kevin Spacey and Macaulay Culkin.
