Career Path

Martha Karua is a prominent Kenyan lawyer who has made significant contributions to the legal profession.

She obtained a law degree from the University of Nairobi and went on to establish a successful legal practice.

Karua has been actively involved in advocating for human rights and the rule of law, fighting for justice and equality.

Political Career

Martha Karua has had a distinguished political career in Kenya, holding various key positions and advocating for good governance and women's rights.

  • She served as the Member of Parliament for Gichugu Constituency from 1997 to 2013, representing the interests of her constituents.
  • Karua has also held several ministerial positions, including Minister of Water Resources Management and Development,
  • She also served as Minister of Justice and Constitutional Affairs, and Minister of Gender and Social Development.
  • Karua was Azimio La Umoja One Kenya Party deputy presidential canditate in 2022

Presidential Candidacy

In 2013, Martha Karua ran for the presidency of Kenya as an independent candidate, becoming the second woman after Charity Ngilu (1997) to contest the highest office in the country.

Her presidential campaign focused on issues such as governance, human rights, and women's empowerment.

Karua came closer to the presidency in 2022 when she was nominated by Azimio One Kenya Alliance to be Raila Odinga’s running mate. They narrowly lost the election to Kenya Kwanza coalition of William Ruto and Rigathi Gachagua (now president and deputy president).

Advocacy for Women's Rights and Social Justice

Throughout her career, Martha Karua has been a vocal advocate for women's rights and social justice.

She has championed gender equality, advocated for the inclusion of women in leadership roles, and fought against discrimination and violence against women.

General Information

Martha Karua, born on September 22, 1957, in Kirinyaga, Kenya, is a prominent Kenyan politician, lawyer, and activist. With a strong background in law, she has actively worked to promote justice, human rights, and good governance.

Karua's political career has seen her hold various ministerial positions and represent her constituents as a Member of Parliament. Her presidential candidacy in 2013 further demonstrated her commitment to democratic processes and women's representation in leadership.

Beyond politics, Martha Karua has been a passionate advocate for women's rights, fighting for gender equality and social justice. Her legal expertise and activism have made her a respected figure in Kenya and an inspiration to many.
