Brands Nike OwnsIncluding Converse

Publish date: 2024-10-02

Nike is known for its iconic sneakers, most notably the omnipresent Air Force 1. Faces of the brand have included sports legends and A-listers like Serena Williams, Drake, and, of course, Michael Jordan. For a deeper dive into the origin story of this fashion powerhouse, we recommend spending a night watching Ben Affleck and Matt Damon's 2023 film Air with a glass of wine in hand. But here, we're highlighting a few of the other brands Nike can claim as its own, including Converse. Keep reading to learn more.

Converse

Back in 2003, Nike dropped $305 million to snag Converse, the brand behind those classic Chuck Taylor All Stars.

Hurley

A year earlier, Nike Inc. added one of the leaders in surf apparel, Hurley, to its portfolio.

Air Jordan

And the Air Jordan brand is another subsidiary that will continue to keep the portfolio thriving.
