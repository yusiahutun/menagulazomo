Losing weight is a goal that many of us strive for, but it can be a difficult process. Meanwhile, for all those who are frantically searching for an easier alternative to shed extra pounds, get your hands on weight loss creams. The best weight loss creams promise to reduce cellulite, tighten the skin, and help you burn calories faster(1). These creams, also known as slimming creams, are helpful in encouraging lymphatic circulation and prevent development of new fat, too.

Talking about different weight loss creams, our contributor Dr. Harikiran Chekuri, Medical Head - ClinicSpots, says, “Fat-burning creams are designed to help boost the metabolism and increase the breakdown of fat cells. They often contain ingredients such as caffeine, green tea extract, and carnitine which are known to stimulate the body's fat-burning processes. These creams can be applied directly to areas of the body where you want to see a fat reduction. Cellulite cream, on the other hand, is specifically designed to target cellulite deposits under the skin. Cellulite creams often contain ingredients that help improve circulation and reduce the appearance of cellulite. However, they do not directly target fat cells. So while both fat-burning and cellulite creams may claim to reduce body fat, they work in different ways and have different intended purposes.”

"Many people apply the cream occasionally, which of course is never going to work. Fat can always return if you ingest more calories and don't exercise, so keep up the good work during and after the 6-12 weeks of application" says Georgios Tzenichristos, cellulite specialist and skincare formulator.

  • Ingredients: The best way to ensure that you’re getting the most out of the weight loss cream is to look for natural ingredients like green tea extract, caffeine, and guarana, that have been proven to help with weight loss.

    How to Use Best Weight Loss Creams?

    • Before applying any weight loss cream, it is important to cleanse the skin first. This will help to remove any dirt, oil, and makeup that may be blocking the pores. Use a gentle cleanser and lukewarm water, and follow up with a toner if desired.
    • Once the skin is clean and dry, it’s time to apply the weight loss cream. Use a circular motion to massage the cream into the skin, ensuring that all areas are evenly covered. Allow the cream to absorb for several minutes before rinsing with lukewarm water.
    • After using the weight loss cream, it is important to moisturize the skin in order to keep it hydrated and healthy. Choose a moisturizer that is suited for your skin type, and use it daily to keep the skin nourished.
    • To get the best results from your weight loss cream, it is important to use it consistently. Try to apply the best weight loss cream twice a day, morning and night, and avoid skipping days.

    FAQ's

    Does a weight loss cream work without exercise?

    A weight loss cream alone may not be enough to achieve significant weight loss without exercise. While the best weight loss creams may help to reduce fat in certain areas of the body, exercise is an integral part of a comprehensive weight loss plan. Regular physical activity and a healthy diet are essential for achieving and maintaining a healthy body weight.

    Can you use slimming cream on your face?

    While slimming creams can be used on the body to reduce fat and cellulite, they should not be used on the face as they can be too harsh and cause skin irritation. Additionally, as they are designed to reduce fat in the body, they can also strip the skin of moisture, causing it to become dry and dull. Therefore, it is best to use a face cream specifically designed for the facial area to keep your skin healthy and hydrated.

    Who should not take fat burner creams?

    Fat burner creams are not suitable for everyone and should only be used under the guidance of a doctor. People with certain medical conditions, such as heart disease or diabetes, should not take fat burner creams, as they may interfere with their medications or worsen their condition. People with a history of skin allergies should also avoid using these creams, as they may cause an allergic reaction. Finally, pregnant or nursing mothers should avoid using fat burner creams due to possible risks to their unborn child.

    Does fat-burning oil work with cream?

    The effectiveness of fat-burning oil when used with cream depends on the specific product and the individual's skin type. While some products may provide temporary results, it is recommended to use the product as directed and to keep track of the results to determine if it is working for you.

    Do belly-slimming creams work?

    Yes, the belly-slimming creams with high concentrations of lipolytic ingredients work wonders.

    Do fat burning creams work?

    Yes, fat burner creams show effective results but they won’t magically dissolve the weight overnight.
