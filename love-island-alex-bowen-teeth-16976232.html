In fact, we can't go a day without an update from the fan favourites, which means it would have been impossible to miss Alex Bowen's announcement about an image overhaul this week.

Alex Bowen took to Instagram to reveal he's decided to have 10 veneers put onto his teeth, in a bid for a celebrity smile.

The tattooed star uploaded a snap of his new gnashers for his 1.1 million fans, praising his cosmetic dentist: "Massive thank you to @ask_the_dentist for giving me my dream smile."

A number of the reality star's fans told Alex he'd "ruined" himself.

One fan ranted: "I LOVED your teeth. They were what made you so gorgeous and were so unique to you. I'm gutted. But you still look hot. Just a bit more generic sadly!"

Sadly, the negative comments didn't stop there, with another fan saying: "Bad example to younger ones defo [sic]."

Another chimed in: "I want veneers but I don't want them to look like this."

However, it wasn't all doom and gloom as a number of Alex's fans rushed in to defend him from the haters.

One loyal follower wrote back: "Guys it's his decision so if your gonna be negative leave and I think they look great."

While a second said: "Bloody gorgeous, Liv is one lucky lady, I actually preferred your own teeth but you're beautiful all the same.

"People shouldn.t comment nasty stuff you work hard if you wanna buy new teeth then why not, hella haters these days."

A third bit back: "Such a beautiful man!! New teeth old teeth doesn't matter you will always be beaut!"

"It's sad that people comment negatively or even suggestively to ruin someone's moment of happiness. Not to mention the fact that Alex isn't going to lose sleep that Sharon or Julie thought he was fitter before.
