Hailey Bieber's Latest Latte Dress Was Super-Short and Super-Sparkly

Publish date: 2024-03-12

When Hailey Bieber finds a trend she loves, she really commits — and her latest latte-inspired ensemble is no exception. Strawberry girl summer? We hardly knew her, because Bieber's parade of brown looks in Paris have put us firmly in an autumnal state of mind. Just yesterday, she wore an LBD by Saint Laurent, B being for brown and not black for Mrs. Bieber this time around, and today, she wore a custom-made Ferragamo creation that has us buzzing more than a double shot of espresso.

The micro-mini featured a deep, dark chocolatey hue and both mesh detailing and crystal embellishment. The back of the dress featured a geometric twisted detail and even though the hemline was super short, the dress featured a thigh slit that was accented by shimmering crystal designs. She finished the look with an architectural, sculpted stiletto heel, and a matching espresso bedazzled brown suede mini Hug pouch from the brand. It was all for another stop on her rhode beauty launch tour, which celebrated her products landing on European shelves (and, presumably, lips).

While Hailey's fashion has been front and center, it's all because of her new rhode peptide lip tint. The product made headlines for being Hailey's first-ever color cosmetic after her namesake brand established itself as a skincare-first company. The lip tint offers a subtle color that the brand describes as "sheer-but-buildable."

Ribbon is a pink hue and, to nobody's surprise, Espresso is the brown shade that launched and caused a full-on chromatic shift to Bieber's wardrobe. Other options include a rosy taupe color called Toast and a deep berry that Bieber named Raspberry Kelly.
