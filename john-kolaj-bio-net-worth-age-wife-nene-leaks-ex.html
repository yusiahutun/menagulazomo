John Kolaj Family

John’s dad passed away in 1969 Italy when he was still very young. His younger brother, Giorgio Kolaj, the youngest of five children was just six months old when that happened.

Six months after this tragedy, their mother, Rosa Kolaj, AKA Mamma Rosa, arrived with them in the United States (New York City) in the spring of 1960. This was the family’s humble beginning of their journey toward the American Dream.

Though the early years were a struggle, John recalls even today how by “God’s grace”, she always had a way of turning the hardships into opportunities of instilling in her kids core values through the lessons of life. Through chores and responsibilities, John and his siblings learned the value of discipline, teamwork, and commitment. We began working early on in their youth, and through the years gained much experience in the pizza industry in New York City. And, by the summer of 1986, they opened their own pizzeria, Famous Famiglia, in the heart of New York City. In the years to follow, they opened several more pizzerias throughout New York City, winning awards for their quality and customer service standards.

John so you know has 3 brothers and a sister. Yet, unfortunately, one of them, brother Tony “Ndue” Kolaj is no more. He passed away on 16 January 2020 at the age of 58. He was a director of quality control at their Famous Famiglia and was living in White Plains.

The remaining Kolaj brother is Paul and he is the President and CEO at their pizza place.
