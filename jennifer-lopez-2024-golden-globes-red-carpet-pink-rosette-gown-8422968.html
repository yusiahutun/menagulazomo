Jennifer Lopez Nailed the Rosette Trend at the 2024 Golden Globes

Publish date: 2024-08-14

Jennifer Lopez is no stranger to the Golden Globes herself (she has two nominations for her roles in Selena and Hustlers, after all). But, at the 2024 Globes, the multi-hyphenate is adding supportive wife to her stacked resume. Lopez arrived on the red carpet Sunday night alongside her husband, Ben Affleck, whose film Air is nominated tonight in the Best Motion Picture - Musical or Comedy category. And even though she's not technically up for an award tonight, Lopez took home the trophy for best dressed.

Lopez stole the show in a strapless Barbie pink gown that featured a corseted bodice and dramatic off-the-shoulder sleeves constructed from giant rosettes. A pink crystal-embellished clutch, a diamond hummingbird ring, and pink diamond drop earrings rounded out her look on the red carpet. As for her beauty look, she styled her hair in Old Hollywood waves parted to the side and finished off her glam with pink eyeshadow and a swipe of lipstick in a matching shade.

Lopez's last appearance at the Globes was back in 2020 when she was nominated for her portrayal of Ramona in the critically acclaimed film Hustlers. Lopez was a literal gift to us all in her adorable green, gold, and white bow dress (perhaps the genesis of the bow renaissance?). She wore her hair in a tight bun wrapped with two thick braids and opted for her go-to glowy bronzed glam.

Last time we saw her at the Globes, Lopez was joined by her then-fiancé, Alex Rodriguez. The two separated in 2021 and Lopez reconnected with Affleck shortly after, ultimately tying the knot in 2022.

The couple has been known to capture the media's attention on red carpets between adorable bouts of PDA and meme-worthy moments (recall his viral sad face at the 2023 Grammys). But Ben and J.Lo were all smiles on Sunday as they walked onto the storied carpet, posing for the flashbulbs.
