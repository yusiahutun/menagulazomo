Who is Tom Selleck?

Tom Selleck, whose zodiac sign is Aquarius, was born on January 29, 1945, in Detroit, Michigan, USA. He is an American citizen.

Tom is an actor, producer, and member of the California Army National Guard. He rose to fame playing Thomas Magnum in the 1980–1988 television series “Magnum, P.I.”

Tom was reared by his real estate investor father Robert Dean Selleck and homemaker mother Martha Selleck in Sherman Oaks, California, as well as Detroit.

Prior to attending Los Angeles Valley College, he attended Grant High School. He later transferred to the University of Southern California but left in his final year to pursue an acting career and study at the Beverly Hills Playhouse.

He received a draft notice during the Vietnam War and enlisted in the 160th Infantry Regiment of the California Army National Guard in 1967, serving in that unit until 1973.

In 1965, he had one of his first TV appearances in the “The Dating Game” TV series, which he returned to in 1967. He started acting in films like “Coma” and “The Seven Minutes” after leaving his infantry regiment, but he didn’t become well-known until he started portraying Lance White, a private eye, in “The Rockford Files” TV series in the 1970s.

He made his film debut in 1972’s “Daughters of Satan,” and in 1979’s “Concrete Cowboys” he received his first lead role. In 1980, he started playing private eye Thomas Magnum in the “Magnum, P.I.” TV series, having to decline the part of Indiana Jones in “Raiders of the Lost Ark,” which eventually went to Harrison Ford.

Due to the success of “Magnum, P.I.,” the Ferrari 308 GTS that Thomas drives in the show has earned the nickname “Magnum” Ferrari.
