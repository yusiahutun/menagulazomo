Julia Fox just wore a belt as a dress at Paris Fashion Week

Publish date: 2024-06-20

Little black dress has a whole new meaning...

    Move over, Diesel. Julia Fox has found a new way to wear a belt and we have to say, it’s pretty mesmerising. 

    She will forever be synonymous with some of the most outrageously avant-garde fashion week looks we’ve ever seen. And the Uncut Gems actress has brought her A-game from NYFW across the pond to Paris, serving yet another slew of unforgettable garms. 

    After channelling ‘free the nipple’ energy at the Courreges show on Tuesday, Julia stepped out in the French capital last night putting on a daring display in a collection of leather belts that formed a mini dress with a strapless neckline, belt buckles that made for a quirky silver panelling and a corset silhouette that hoisted her chest.

    Julia at PFW AW23

    MORE: Julia Fox’s 9 craziest New York Fashion Week 2023 looks

    She paired the dress with a longline leather jacket and black court shoes with silver detailing – a pared back ensemble by Julia’s fashion week standards. Her ensemble paired with her stunning fiery red tresses was giving major 'matrix-chic' vibes. 

    Garments that are intended to be worn across the waist area seem to be Julia’s favourite clothing to upcycle.Before her bold belt look, the undisputed queen of upcycling donned a dress constructed from jean waistbands at Couture week, patchworked together into an asymmetric one-shoulder mini dress offering some major sideboob action.

    Her dress was made from leather belts

    After nailing a plethora of stellar ensembles at Couture week, we thought she may have stopped there… but that was just the beginning for the 32 year old who has been on a spree of serving stellar looks across the whole of fashion month. Never one to shy away from the limelight, the Uncut Gems star donned major looks both on and off the runway, with a few pivotal moment’s she probably wants to forget.

    MORE: Julia Fox steps out in her craziest custom denim look ever

    NYFW was eventful for the actress, but despite the raised eyebrows after her Alexander Wang walk and her runway slip up during Luis De Javier’s show, Julia kept her signature experimental style intact, giving sartorial fans exactly what they came to the Big Apple for with an epic line up of head-turning ‘lewks’.

    Kanye West’s ex-girlfriend then had a well deserved break during London Fashion Week before coming back to Paris like she’d never left for this week’s shows.

    Love it or hate it (FYI, we’re obsessed), she never misses when making a statement.
