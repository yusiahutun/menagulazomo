BBC Breakfast fans in awe of Tina Daheley as she shares glamorous snap

Publish date: 2024-03-01

BBC Breakfast fans swooned over relief presenter Tina Daheley.

The journalist is best known as the main newsreader on The Radio 2 Breakfast Show with Zoe Ball, however she occasionally fills in on the corporation's flagship live sofa programme.

But as she took to social media, fans could not help but share adoration for the broadcasting personality.

Tina is a fan favourite on the show and although she doesn't appear daily on the famous sofa, viewers always love it when she does join.
