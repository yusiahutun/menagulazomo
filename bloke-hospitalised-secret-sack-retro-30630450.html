Builder put in hospital after hidden adult mag stash falls from ceiling

Publish date: 2024-08-02

A builder was treated in hospital after a hidden stash of retro porn magazines fell on him during a house renovation.

The labourer was part of a small crew doing work on a period townhouse in south eat London when a sack of jazz mags came tumbling from the eaves of the attic when the roof was removed.

The story was revealed by Ben Hall on Twitter, now known as X, during a chat about the work he did on the house, namely removing the roof and every floor and re-building them in steel.
