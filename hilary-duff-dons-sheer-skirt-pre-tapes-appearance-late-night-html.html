Hilary Duff emerged from her Manhattan hotel on Thursday wearing a black tea-length skirt so sheer it revealed her panties beneath.

'I know with my journey with [my son Luca Cruz Comrie] it's like, "Come on be tough! Get up! You're okay!" That was like something I constantly heard and I'm like, "Maybe he's not okay right now."

'Who am I to say that he should be okay or to tell him that in that moment he's not? And he's going to learn how to keep himself in control when he's older, but right now why am why are we going to try to squash that and bury it...I want him to feel the power of of his whole self.'

The Texan-born blonde mothered the 11-year-old boy during her six-year marriage to Mike Comrie, which ended in 2016.

Hilary and her second husband - Winnetka Bowling League frontman Matthew Koma - are proud parents of five-year-old daughter Banks Violet Bair and two-year-old daughter Mae James Bair.

Duff was still technically married to the 43-year-old retired NHL player when she met the 36-year-old Breakfast for Dinner singer while collaborating on her 2015 album, Breathe In. Breathe Out.

The OLLY paid partner and Matthew might have split for six months in 2017, but they will celebrate their fourth wedding anniversary on December 21.
