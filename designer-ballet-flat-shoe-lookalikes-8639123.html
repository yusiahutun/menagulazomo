I Found Lookalikes for These 5 Designer Ballet Flats on Amazon

Publish date: 2024-05-24

Designer fashion is almost always a part of the style conversation because of its quality craftsmanship, creative designs, and luxurious allure. While people may be able to secure a few designer goods here and there, it may not be feasible to snag countless opulent pieces on the regular. Therefore, if you’re on the hunt for coveted designer flats from Chanel, Miu Miu, and Alaïa for less, the below five Amazon lookalikes may spark your interest.     

5 Designer Lookalike Flats

Vivalaver Cap-Toe Ballet Flat

Meghan Markle wears Chanel flats like clockwork, though the brand’s two-toned design captured my attention. Available for $1,245, the Chanel cap-toe ballerina flats combine sophistication and practicality into one style, so, I was pretty excited when I noticed Amazon had a similar version for $51. The Vivalaver Ballet Flat includes a dainty front bow, slightly raised heel, and black and beige color scheme. In other words, you get the luxurious look for $1,194 less. 

Arqa Mesh Mary Jane Ballet Flat

It seems like everyone from Jennifer Lawrence to Jennifer Lopez has been wearing mesh flats, making a case for the see-through footwear—and Alaïa is behind one of the most popular toe-baring designs. The popular bold shoe retails for $890, including a Mary Jane silhouette, buckle, and smooth rim. Amazon, on the other hand (or should I say foot) has a $58 iteration from Arqa, featuring the same overall composition and even comes in gold and beige.   

Circus NY by Sam Edelman Zuri Ballet Flat

Miu Miu took traditional ballet shoes and made them acceptable to wear out in the streets with its Satin Bow Ballerina Flats, which cost $950. Circus NY by Sam Edelman put its own spin on the style by adding criss-cross top straps—and the shoes are $80. For $870 less, you can enjoy a similar round toe, gathered front ruching, gold-tone hardware, and an adorable bow. Plus, you can shop the piece in 13 designs.    

Steve Madden Vinetta Mary Jane Flat

If you feel like you’ve seen these Alaïa leather studded flats before, you’re not wrong. Rihanna has worn them on six occasions, and I would, too, thanks to the shoe’s cool-girl design. The only problem? They’re $1,250. Thankfully, Steve Madden is coming in clutch by offering an on-sale $60 Mary Jane style that embodies similar qualities. Look forward to a thin strap, slim buckle, raised heel, and all-over rhinestone studs.     

Fericzot Buckle Slip-On Mules

Whenever I hear Manolo Blahnik, I instantly think of Carrie Bradshaw. Unfortunately, not everyone has a book deal and weekly column like Bradshaw, so the $54 Fericzot Buckle Mules will have to do. They’re nearly identical to the $795 Manolo Blahnik Maysale Suede Mules, making a pretty good case for the affordable Amazon pair. Shop the shoes in 10 hues and consider a taller version to separate yourself from the pack.
