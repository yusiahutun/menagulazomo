Anthony LaPaglia marries Alexandra Henkel in Hawaii

Publish date: 2024-09-08

Anthony LaPaglia, 59, has married his partner of three years, Alexandra Henkel, 29. 

The Without a Trace star exchanged vows with Alexandra in Hawaii on April 28, a year after getting engaged.

Announcing the news on Instagram, the blushing bride wrote: '28.4.2018. The day I married the love of my life.'
