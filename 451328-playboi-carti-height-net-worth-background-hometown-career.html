Playboi Carti’s biography

The singer was born on September 13, 1996 in Riverdale, Georgia, but raised in Fairburn, Georgia. He grew up in a financially disadvantaged family, which saw him struggle a lot in his childhood. As a result, he found himself in bad company to the point that he couldn't heed his mother's words.

Career

An argument with his high school basketball coach made Carter stop basketball and opt for music instead. He would skip his classes to work on his music or at H&M to support himself.

Even though he barely graduated high school due to skipping classes, his relentless in music paved the way for a bright future. By 2011 he was rapping using the stage name Sir Cartier. In 2013 he changed the name to Playboi Carti.

Career beginnings

After deciding to pursue music full-time, Playboi Carti moved to New York with some family members. While there, he went to stay at a drug dealer's house, and that is how he started interacting with members of the A$AP Mob who would frequent the house, one of them being A$AP Bari.

He struck a friendship with Bari, a founding member of the A$AP group, and introduced him to Rocky. Rocky started mentoring Carti and helped his 2015 singles like Broke Boi and Fetti attract attention.

Most of his singles at the time were published on SoundCloud. During this phase of his career, he frequently collaborated with many artists from Atlanta's underground rap scene, including:

  • UnoTheActivist who is his cousin
  • Yung Bans
  • Thouxanbanfauni
  • Ethereal
  • Lil Yachty

He began to tour with Lil Uzi and A$AP Ferg when Interscope Records signed him.

Debut Mixtape and A$AP Mob

Carti’s eponymous debut mixtape came out in April 2017 and attracted several music publications such as:

  • Pitchfork
  • XXL
  • Spin
  • PopMatters
  • HotNewHipHop

It peaked at number 12 on the Billboard 200, with the single Magnolia reaching number 29 on the Billboard Hot 100. Around the time Carti was named one of the XXL’s 2017 Freshman Class, A$AP Mob featured him in their single Raf.

His debut full-length album, Die Lit, was released in May 2018, and it peaked at number 3 on the US Billboard 200. There were guest appearances from Bryson Tiller, Nicki Minaj, and Skepta, among many others. He has been influenced by Mob not only in music but also in fashion.

Dating history and children

In 2017 Playboi was in a brief relationship with American model Blac Chyna. After that, he had another short stint with Rubi Rose. Rubi is a rapper and model who gained fame after being featured in the music video, Bad and Boujee by Migos. Carti was accused of shooting a gun at Rubi after she hid his phones before a flight.
