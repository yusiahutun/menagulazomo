Coachella 2019 Explosion &amp; Fire

Publish date: 2024-10-03
  • Festivalgoers shared video footage on social media when a fire broke out at Coachella 2019.
  • The CAL FIRE and Riverside County Fire Department was called to the festival to take care of the blaze.
  • Attendees reported hearing an explosion on one of the campsites, with The Sun claiming that people were spotted fleeing from one of the shower blocks.

Coachella 2019 Weekend 1 has only just started, but the drama has already arrived.

Related Story

After an unforgettable first night, which featured performances by Janelle Monáe and Childish Gambino, as well as guest appearances by Cardi B and Selena Gomez during DJ Snake's set, a fire broke out on the Coachella site.

The explosion, which quickly turned into a fire, was enough to wake some revelers. As Twitter user @justxdamian wrote, "the explosion was about 60ft away from our tent... all seriousness this wasn’t a cool thing to wake up to. Took this as we ran away #coachella #fire #explosion":

Meanwhile, @allisynrees wrote, "Explosion in Coachella camp grounds at 2 AM, looks like propane tank exploded."

The CAL FIRE and Riverside County Fire Department attended the site and posted a report of the incident on Twitter writing, "2:28 am - Fire Contained. Mobile Shower Unit in Lot 8 Storage Area. Total of 2 trailers involved. 1 damaged, 1 destroyed. No reported injuries to fire personnel or civilians. Fire crews will remain on scene for 1 hour for overhaul. #Coachella":

Basically, Coachella 2019 has only just begun, but the iconic festival is already bringing the drama.
