Sebastian Maniscalco Bio, Age, Height, Wife, Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-01-29

Sebastian Maniscalco is an Italian- American stand-up comedian, Actor, Writer, and Producer. Some of his works are Sebastian Says, Sabastian Maniscalco: Aren’t You Embarrassed? , Sebastian Maniscalco: What’s wrong with People?, Tag, Cruise, The House, and many other shows. Currently, he is filming for his projects which will be released soon and are titled About My Father, Untitled Super Mario Project, and Spinning Gold. He has also shared a tour announcement of Nobody Does This.

Early Life

Sebastian Maniscalco was born on 8th July 1973 in Arlington Heights, Illinois, United States. The names of his parents are Salvatore Maniscalco and Rose Maniscalco. He has a sister whose name is Jessica Maniscalco Dadon. At the age of 15, he came to the USA with his family. He used to enjoy most of his time by spending quality time with his family. Sebastian went to Rolling Meadows High School for graduation and further went to Northern Illinois University in 1995. Over there he graduated with a degree in Communication Studies.

Sebastian Maniscalco Career

Sebastian moved to Los Angeles and performed at Open mics in bars, bowling alleys, and worked as a waiter at Four Season Hotel in Beverly Hill, California from 1998 to 2005. In between, he was also working to build his career in the entertainment industry. In 2004 he worked as an actor in a TV series named Complete Savages as Dance Club Manager.

Later he appeared in TV series named The Very Funny Show in 2009 and also worked on a short film named Pro-Semitism Law in 2011. In 2016 he appeared in a TV movie named Sebastian Says as himself. Next year he was seen in The House as a Stand-up comic and a voice role of Johnny in The Nut Job 2: Nutty by Nature. As a producer, he has released works like Comedy Central Presents, Sebastian Maniscalco: What’s wrong with people? and Sebastian Maniscalco: Aren’t You Embarrassed?

In 2018 he worked on many projects like Cruise as Dinardo, Tag as Pastor, and Green Book as Johnny Venere. Next year he was seen as Crazy Joe Gallo in The Irishman. In 2021 his show Well Done Sebastian is also streaming on discovery plus. Later in 2022, he was seen in Somewhere in Queens and as completed the filming of Spinning Gold where he is seen as Giorgio Moroder. Currently, his work like About my Father and Untitled Supper Mario Project has been postponed and will soon begin filming.
