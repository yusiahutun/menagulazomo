Teddy Swims makes quite the fashion statement at star-studded pre-Grammys party

Publish date: 2024-06-15

Singer-songwriter Teddy Swims attended the pre-Grammys party for this year’s ceremony and made quite a statement with his pink suit.

Teddy, whose birth name is Jaten Collin Dimsdale, rose to fame thanks to covers he posted to his YouTube channel (including ones of Michael Jackson and Stevie Wonder). The celebrity eventually landed himself a major record deal. Since then, he’s released his debut album and has attended award shows. This includes the Grammys, which he attended last year and has recently made waves with by attending a pre-party for this year’s ceremony.

Teddy Swims’ pre-Grammy party pink suit

Teddy Swims attended a 2024 pre-Grammys party at Citizen News in Los Angeles, California yesterday (February 1) and made quite a statement with his fashion choices. He wore a satin pink suit. The viral sensation’s suit consisted of hot pink trousers and a jacket paired with a satin black shirt.

His shoes added to the look, as the black sneakers were covered in white stars.

He accessorized with some baby pink glasses. He also wore a black belt to help keep up his trousers.

The very tattooed music star was pictured at the Warner Music-hosted party with many high-profile names. They included the likes of Barbie soundtrack producer Mark Ronson and R&B singer Janelle Monáe. He was also pictured performing for those in attendance.

Teddy, 31, isn’t just known for his bold and bright fashion choices though as he recently went back to his music beginnings and performed a cover of a viral hit by a huge pop star, and it had fans obsessed.

Teddy has everyone obsessed with his Taylor Swift cover

Teddy recently covered Taylor Swift’s 2019 hit Cruel Summer for BBC Radio 1. The station often has musicians in the studio to sing one of their own songs and a cover. There have been many memorable ones, including Harry Styles covering Lizzo, Miley Cyrus’ cover of Lana Del Rey’s Summertime Sadness, and Alicia Keys covering Billie Eilish.

His session and cover have fans absolutely obsessed. The song has recently been having a resurgence on TikTok so it’s no surprise he picked it.
