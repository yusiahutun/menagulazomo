Every car available in ACC

Publish date: 2024-04-09

We're bringing you the complete roster of raceable vehicles in Kunos' racing simulator.

The Assetto Corsa Competizione car list is a big one! KUNOS have gone all out by including an extensive list of cars for their latest racing simulator.

Assetto Corsa Competizione has been out since 2019 and the developers are continuously working to expand the library of cars at players' disposal.

Table of Contents

  • Assetto Corsa Competizione Car List
  • GT3 Cars
  • GT4 Cars

ACC is also the officially licenced game of the 24 Hours of Spa and GT3 and GT4 Blanchipain Series for 2018 and 2019. There's plenty on offer - here's the full car list!

Assetto Corsa Competizione Car List

To make it easy to see which type each car in the game belongs to, we've broken the ACC racers up into the different series and categories that they belong to.

There are some seriously quick cars on offer and they originate from a variety of manufacturers and countries. The main two classifications on offer are cars from the GT3 and GT4 categories.

In total, there are over 100 cars in ACC and we'll begin with the GT3 cars that the game is most famous for.

GT3 Cars

The GT3 cars make up the lion's share of cars within Assetto Corsa Competizione. The GT3 cars come from the GT World Challenge, Blanchipan GT Series, British GT Championship and the Intercontinental GT Challenge.
