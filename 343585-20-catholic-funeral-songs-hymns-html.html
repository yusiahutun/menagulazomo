20 best Catholic funeral songs and hymns

Publish date: 2024-02-12

Death is an inevitable part of human life. Consequently, losing a loved one brings up the emotions of sadness, not to mention that eventually, you will have to hold a funeral in honour of the departed. Different people mourn differently, and the emotions they feel during this time vary from time to time. Music is a powerful tool for people who are grieving, and hence the preference for religious funeral songs in funerals. For instance, Catholic funeral songs help mourners process grief differently.

Hymns are among the top choice of music for Catholic funerals. Luckily, there are different hymns that one can choose for the burial. The different Catholic funeral songs and hymns evoke emotions such as joy and comfort among a community. This type of music also brings the community together as they sing in oneness.

What songs are sung at Catholic funerals?

Catholic burials vary from others in that the funeral celebration takes a communal nature. Thus, it becomes critical to select the tracks to sing at the funeral carefully. Often, funeral planning involves having a meeting with the people who will participate in the funeral actively. The mass celebration has standard music, but besides that, there are other parts that music fits. These parts that require the music for catholic funeral mass include:

The processional hymn

Processional hymns refer to the songs that the congregants sing during the opening of the burial celebration. There are multiple song choices for the opening hymn that you can choose.

Responsorial hymn

This hymn comes right after the reading of Bible readings for the day. The typical responsorial psalms for catholic funerals come are Psalm 23, Psalm 27, and Psalm 103. Only one responsorial psalm is required in a funeral service.

Communion hymn

This song is sung during the receiving of the Lord's table. Some of the catholic funeral hymns that are fit for communion time include how great thou art, Here I Am, Lord, Come to Me, and Blest are They.

Recessional hymn

This song comes at the end of the mass celebration. It serves as the concluding song. Some of the melodies that can serve as recessional hymns include You are Mine, The Strife is O'er, On Eagles Wings, and The Prayer of St. Francis

Irish Catholic funeral songs

Irish songs are famous for the rich culture and ancient heritage that they aim to perpetuate. Ireland is known as a country with rich spirituality, and the music depicts the same. Some of the songs that are sung in Irish funerals include "O Breathe on Me O Breath of God.", "Be Thou My Vision", and "May the Road Rise to Meet You".

The different parties will give you their input regarding the choice of songs for the burial service.
