Nicole was born and raised in Mumbai, Maharashtra. She was born on 23rd of October in 1989. Nicole fell in love with the art of dance since a young age. She has one younger sibling, Ria Concessao who is 26 year old and is working in Ontario, Canada. She along with her team mate Sonal Devraj, started their YouTube channel Team Naach in 2014.

She completed her schooling from RN Podar School in Mumbai and has done her graduation from  Smt. Mithibai Motiram Kundnani College of Commerce and Economics in Mumbai, India.

Career

Nicole says her biggest milestone thus far achieved, is performing at the YouTube Fan Fest of 2019. It was at that stage when she realized the power of social media and met her virtual family in person. Nicole is well trained in  Kathak, Bollywood dance and Jive. But she performs other dance forms too.

Before becoming a dancer, Nicole used to be an agent, brand manager and Public Relations Officer. Later, she decided to quit her job and follow her passion of dancing. She has gained over 4 millions subscribers on her YouTube channel.

She also did work in Pre-wedding shoots and Sangeet ceremonies. She has been posting her content on a regular basis since she debuted her channel Team Naach on YouTube. She first started as a dance teacher in a small studio with 5-10 students but she has come a long way and is now a successful choreographer with more than 5000 students now. Also, she has been featured in Filter copy’s video, When you Live to Dance.

In 2020, Nicole started her own YouTube channel and has gained more than 170K subscribers till now. She has gained recognition through her talent and great performance in dance. She has done dance videos with many celebrities including Nora Fatehi. She has also appeared with her team mate Sonal on interview with Zoom channel.
