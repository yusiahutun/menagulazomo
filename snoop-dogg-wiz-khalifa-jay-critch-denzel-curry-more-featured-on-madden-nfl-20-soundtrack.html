Snoop Dogg, Wiz Khalifa, Jay Critch, Denzel Curry &amp; More Featured on Madden NFL 20 Soundtrack

Publish date: 2024-05-15

CALIFORNIA (CelebrityAccess) – EA Sports has revealed the Madden 20 soundtrack, which includes 22 original songs, featuring the likes of Snoop Dogg, Wiz Khalifa, Jay Critch and Denzel Curry among others.

For this year’s soundtrack, each of the artists were asked to create songs specifically inspired by Madden which will be exclusive to the beloved video game.

“For the first time ever, a soundtrack has been specifically built with Madden NFL and football as the foundation for the aesthetic of the sound, the tone, and the feel,” said EA Sports in a statement. “This groundbreaking move brings next-level creative to redefine the sound of Madden for seasons to come. The soundscape that permeates your headphones or speakers will also evoke a breadth of emotions while you play.

“Whether you’re in preparation mode, honing your starting lineup, celebrating a big win, or on the edge of your couch in an urgent high-stakes situation with the clock ticking away,” the statement continued, “this soundtrack has a song that speaks to every situation.”

According to EA Sports, the company has sold more than 130 million copies of Madden games since the franchise debuted back in 1988.

Madden 20 will be available on August 2, but in the meantime, you can check out the full soundtrack via Spotify.
