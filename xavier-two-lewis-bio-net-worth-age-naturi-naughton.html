Xavier “Two” Lewis Job

Xavier “Two” Lewis got his start as a popular skater, dancer & athlete. A hobby to throw parties sprouted inside him and with the assistance of his friends and leveraging off of his popularity, he established his first company MPG. MPG gained the attention of a few people in the industry that eventually connected him with Eddie “Skeeter Rock” Weathers. Eddie was the head of A&R at SoSoDef and Chadron “Nitti Beatz” Moore was the CEO of Playmaker Music.

They took him under their wing. Xavier had never thought of becoming a part of the music industry but he did. He left the industry after a short stint & on his break to becoming a Music Executive, he explored many different career paths from clothing designer, sales, medicine, to be a chef, a teacher, a coach and almost being a part of the Olympics before a devasting injury that ended that portion of my career.

Furthermore, he told VoyageATL, “Trust the Process,” I always say when reflecting on my journey, “because if you take small steps every day toward the goals you have at hand, then you will be able to accomplish everything you want.” My relentless pursuit to make my goals a reality led me to meet and work with some of the entertainment industry’s top professionals such as Omari Hardwick, Remy Ma, Dionne Warwick, Big Daddy Kane, and Jermaine Dupri, Manuel Seal & more.”

In addition, in 2017, Xavier moved to NY to become the COO at The Vinyl Crown Collective. He ran a premier studio & office space in midtown Manhattan. He and his team worked with other artists such as Christian Combs, Waka Flaka, Dave East, Rich The Kid, Smoke Purpp and H.E.R. Who’s album won a Grammy.

After his success, he decided to start another company, the Owner’s District Management Company (@ownersdistrict). Speaking about his company, he shared, “Owners District is a Management Agency. Owner’s District boasts an exceptional roster of talent including Omari Hardwick (Starz Power), Marco Mckinnis (Republic Records), Caleb Steph (Dirty Hit), Benjamin Earl Turner, Lexxy, Mike Dollas, & a host of talented producers.”

Moreover, Xavier added further, “Owner’s District also has curated special events & listening parties with Akoo, Hustle Gang, ASAP Rocky & Block Boy JB, NYE with Teyana Taylor, Super Bowl Llll with Teyana, Cardi B, Gucci Mane, Kevin Hart, & 50 Cent.”

The company specializes in project management.

Furthermore, Xavier also runs a nonprofit named On Track Georgia (@trackgeorgia). The mission is to help kids stay on track in life. They have a track team a subsidiary “in which we provide kids with a means to learn how to run, stay in shape, learn how to get mentally stronger & other valuable lessons under the leadership of @koachcarl & @mr_itmattersnot.”

According to his IMDB, he worked as a producer for several music videos, TV shows, and TV specials.

Fun Fact: Xavier speaks Korean and attended Georgia Institute of Technology and Kyungpook National University in Deagu Korea.
