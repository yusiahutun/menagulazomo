Prince William Jokes About Hair Loss

Publish date: 2024-08-03

Prince William isn't afraid to crack a joke about his hair (or lack thereof). Today, while performing a royal duty in a London coffee shop, the father of two shook hands with hairdressers from the barbershop next door, People reports.

"I don’t have much hair, I can’t give you much business," he quipped to one of them.

The 35-year-old royal was out to meet with Spitafields Crypt Trust, a charity that supports those dealing with homelessness and addiction. Even the coffee shop where the meeting was held, Paper & Cup, supports the good cause by offering a "training ground" for those in recovery, according to the site.

The Prince's comments didn't stop at the door, however. During his visit, he was congratulated for expecting his third child with Kate Middleton. In response, he said to one midwife, "All the best with the midwifery, I may see you sooner than you think."

After the meeting, the Prince received a wooden owl as a gift for his newborn—and two more for Prince George and Princess Charlotte, so they wouldn't get jealous. "We don’t want arguments," he said.
