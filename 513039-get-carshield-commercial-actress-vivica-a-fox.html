Early life

Vivica Anjanetta Fox was born on July 30, 1964, making her 58 years old (as of 2022). She is the daughter of William and Everlyena Fox. Her father, a school administrator, passed away in 2017, while her mother is a drug technician. Fox also has three siblings, 2 brothers (William and Marvin Fox) and a sister (Alecia Williams).

The CarShield commercial actress was born in South Bend, Indiana. She spent most of her childhood in Indianapolis, where she attended Arlington High School. After graduating high school, she left for Los Angeles to follow her dream. She enrolled in Golden West College and graduated with an Associate's degree in Social Sciences.

Career beginnings

While in L.A., the CarShield commercial actress got a job working at a restaurant on Sunset Boulevard. She also worked as a dancer and even appeared in the musical TV show Soul Train and in the music video Meeting in the Ladies Room, the hit single by R&B all-female group, Klymaxx.

Marriage and dating history

What does the CarShield Commercial actress’ personal life look like? Is she married? Vivica Fox is single and happy. However, she has been in a few relationships and was once married.

Fox married singer Christopher Harvest, aka, Sixx-Nine, in 1998. However, their marriage was short-lived, and they divorced four years later. In an interview with Essence Magazine, the actress revealed that the marriage fell apart because she was the one who was paying for everything. She said:

He didn’t go out and then pursue things with the same drive that I had for my career. You know, a woman doesn’t like paying all the bills all the time. I can only ask for help so many times before I had to come to the conclusion that I didn’t want to be the breadwinner in this family. My mother didn’t raise me to take care of a man. And that was the deciding factor.
