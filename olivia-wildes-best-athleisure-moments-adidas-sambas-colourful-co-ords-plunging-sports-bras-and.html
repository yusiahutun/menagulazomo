Olivia Wildes best athleisure moments: from Adidas Sambas to colourful co-ords of plunging sports

Publish date: 2024-04-28

Olivia Wilde takes her workouts seriously – and she makes sure she looks good doing it.

While the actress and director doesn’t have a regimented workout routine, she has revealed in the past that she regularly attends spin classes in Los Angeles to stay active, per Women’s Health.

And the spin classes are certainly paying off! The 39-year-old-actress is often seen headed to the gym in outfits that show off her sculpted abs and toned legs. While Wilde’s athletic physique has garnered a lot of attention, these days fans are obsessed with her effortlessly chic gym outfits.

Here are some of the Don’t Worry Darling director’s best athleisure moments to inspire your next workout ensemble.

Olivia Wilde’s many, many gym co-ords

Remodelling your engagement ring isn’t rude, it just reflects where you are in life

The actress looked chic stepping out of the gym in Los Angeles in this onesie with an eye-catching pop of red.

Wilde certainly doesn’t shy away from bringing colour to the gym: in September last year, she layered a grey crop top over a metallic purple set that showed off her midriff.

It seems the actress is also the proud owner of a Stanley cup that has the internet in a chokehold.

The House star flaunted her physique in a purple set with a low neckline and high-waisted leggings.

The Cowboys & Aliens actress occasionally likes to switch things up by wearing her usual workout set with a matching tote to synchronise her ensemble.

Back in beige? Tan suits are having an unlikely renaissance

Layers for winter

Amid January’s colder weather, the actress was seen keeping warm on her way to the gym with a fleece-lined white hoodie and a pair of black leggings.

For one of Wilde’s most recent gym outfits, on January 17, she layered a denim jacket over an oversized pink jumper. Her Stanley cup and Fjällräven Kånken backpack in matching teal completed her look.
