DMZ Missions Guide in Warzone

Publish date: 2024-03-28

Salvager is a new Season 2 DMZ Mission for the faction Crown featured in the DMZ mode of Call of Duty: Warzone 2.

In this guide, you can find all the mission's objectives and the rewards you'll obtain by completing it.

Salvager Mission Info

  • Tier 2
  • Release Season 2
  • Faction Crown
  • Rewards Wavebreaker (Vehicle Skin)
    +15.000 XP

Salvager Mission Tasks:

  • Extract the 3 contraband packages from the Sunken Ships in Al Mazrah in 1 deployment
  • Extract the 2 contraband packages from the Sunken Ships in Ashika Island in 1 deployment
