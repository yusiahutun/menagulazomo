Trans-Nzoia county postal codes and ZIP for all regions

Publish date: 2024-02-27

You must include a postal code when sending a letter or parcel to any region in Trans-Nzoia County through the Kenyan postal system. A postal code is the only tool that the postal system has at its disposal to sort the letter or parcel for delivery to its destination. But do you know Trans-Nzoia county postal codes for all regions?

Trans Nzoia County number 026 is one of the counties located in the former Rift Valley Province, Kenya. Kitale is the county headquarters and the largest town in the county. The county is home to some of the most famous tourist attractions in Kenya. These include Saiwa Swamp National Park, Mt. Elgon National Park, and Kitale Museum.

Trans-Nzoia County postal codes

The postal corporation has divided Trans-Nzoia County into twelve different regions. Each region has got its own unique postal code that you need to use if you intend to send a letter or parcel through the postal system. If you don't know the postal codes for the regions in the county, you shouldn't fret. Here is a list of the regions and their respective postal codes.

Kitale postal code

Kitale is famous for its status as Trans-Nzoia County HQ. It is the largest town in the county. The agricultural town is located at the foot of Mount Elgon, about 72 km northwest of Eldoret. According to the 2019 census, the town has a population of more than 162,000 people. What is the P.O. Box for Kitale? When sending a parcel or letter to Kitale, you need to use the postal code 30200.

Kiminini postal code

Kiminini is one of the regions in Trans-Nzoia County. The small but busy agricultural town is located approximately 20 km from Kitale town along Kitale-Webuye Road. If you want to send mail or parcel to Kiminini, you need to use the assigned postal code. The postal code for Kiminini is 30209.

Trans-Nzoia county address

You may need to contact the Trans-Nzoia county government to bid for a tender, apply for a job, or any other reason. If you need to contact the county government, there are several tools that you can use. You can visit the county offices in Kitale town to submit your queries or application.

But what should you do if you live far away from Kitale town? You can write a letter or send an email to the county government. When writing a letter to the county government, you need to know the postal address for the county offices. The postal address for the County Government of Trans-Nzoia is P.O. Box 4211-30200, Kitale, Kenya.

If you are planning to send a letter or parcel to any of the above regions via the postal system, ensure that you use the right postal code.
