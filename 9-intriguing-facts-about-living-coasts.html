Living Coasts is an award-winning coastal zoo and aquarium experience.

Located in Torquay, England, Living Coasts is a unique attraction that offers visitors the chance to explore and learn about coastal wildlife in a stunning setting. This coastal zoo and aquarium has garnered numerous accolades for its conservation efforts and immersive exhibits.

The main focus of Living Coasts is on marine and coastal species.

From playful penguins and graceful seals to vibrant fish and fascinating invertebrates, Living Coasts showcases a diverse range of marine and coastal animals. Visitors can observe these creatures up close and gain a deeper understanding of their habitats and behaviors.

The zoo boasts an impressive collection of penguins.

Living Coasts is known for its colony of penguins, which includes species like the African penguin and the macaroni penguin. The zoo provides a naturalistic environment for these delightful birds, offering them plenty of space to swim, dive, and interact with each other.

Living Coasts supports various conservation initiatives.

Beyond being a popular tourist attraction, Living Coasts plays a vital role in conservation efforts. The facility is committed to protecting threatened and endangered species, participating in breeding programs, and raising awareness about the importance of coastal ecosystems.

Living Coasts offers memorable interactive experiences.

Visitors to Living Coasts have the opportunity to engage in unique experiences, such as feeding the penguins, interacting with playful seals, and attending educational talks and presentations. These hands-on encounters leave a lasting impression and foster a greater appreciation for marine life.

The facility is focused on education and research.

Living Coasts is dedicated to promoting understanding and appreciation of coastal environments through educational programs. The facility also collaborates with research institutions to contribute to marine conservation and share valuable insights into the behavior and biology of coastal species.

Living Coasts boasts stunning coastal views.

Situated in a picturesque location overlooking the sea, Living Coasts offers visitors breathtaking views of the coastline. The expansive windows and outdoor viewing areas allow guests to marvel at the beauty of the surroundings while observing the charismatic marine animals.

Living Coasts is involved in local community outreach.

The zoo actively engages with the local community, organizing events, and providing educational resources to schools in the area. Living Coasts aims to inspire and empower individuals to become stewards of the coast and contribute to the protection of marine habitats.

Living Coasts is a member of the Whitley Wildlife Conservation Trust.

Living Coasts is part of the esteemed Whitley Wildlife Conservation Trust, which also operates Paignton Zoo and Newquay Zoo. This collaboration strengthens conservation efforts and allows for knowledge-sharing and expertise among the different facilities.

In conclusion, Living Coasts offers a truly unique and enriching experience for visitors.
