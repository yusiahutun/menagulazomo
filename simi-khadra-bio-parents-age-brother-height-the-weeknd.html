The Truth Behind Simi Khadra And The Weeknd’s Relationship

The news outlet reported, “The Weeknd and Simi are not dating. They’ve been friends for years.”

In September 2021, an insider had revealed to E! News that they have been enjoying each other’s company and that’s as far as it ever went. The source confirmed, “They are not dating. They have mutual interests and are enjoying putting their ideas together to see what they can come up with. They have been recently talking about partnering on a few humanitarian projects together. He loves talking about movies and the art form.”

Simi was also spotted at The Weeknd’s album release party for Dawn FM in Jan 2022. Delilah in West Hollywood hosted the event. Naturally, there were whispers about Khadra and the artist.

But, fans have been rooting for the two and couldn’t shake off the thoughts that they aren’t dating each other.

Fans theorized that the song was about Angelina Jolie although he didn’t name her explicitly. All the references were made clear after he was spotted sharing a meal with Angelina.

“Call Out My Name” singer has previously dated Selena Gomez and Bella Hadid.

Simi Khadra Brother

Everyone knows by now that Simi Khadra has an identical twin sister named Haze Khadra (real name Haya Abu Khadra). But, she also has an older brother named Fai Khadra. Fai is a social media influencer, a model, and a musician. He has a staggering 2.4 million followers on Instagram. He has collaborated with sunglass brand Oliver Peoples and launched exclusive sunglasses with the influential mindset of Fai.

Fai once sparked a dating rumor after hanging out with Kendall Jenner. Kendall uploaded a photo of herself and Khadra dressed up for the wedding, both debunking and fueling the dating rumors with her caption: “We don’t date he’s just my date.”

As a creative director and artist, Fai has also interviewed Sotheby’s about his love of contemporary art and being a minimalist art heart.

On 9 September 2020, the twin sisters wished happy birthday via IG post, “Happy birthday @yourboyfai !!!!!!!!! We love you so much. our triplet but the favorite child! And the best brother we could have ever asked for LOVEYOULOVEYOULOVEYOUUU”.

They also have a younger brother named Noor Khadra, a polo player. In August 2021 Noor and his team won the Harrison cup at Cowdray Park Polo Club.

Who Are Simi Khadra Parents?

Simi Khadra was born to his art collector father Ahmad Abu Khadra and her mother Rula Khadra, owner of the upscale lifestyle boutique, The Art of Living in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. The girls have featured their father a few times on their social. His last appearance on their IG was in June 2019.

The twin DJs credit their mother for most informing their style. “She taught us about what’s important: quality, material, cuts. She taught us what looks right,” says Simi revealed to Interview Magazine.

In April 2020, she celebrated their mom’s birthday with an IG post, “Happy birthday mom. The quintessence of grace and composure, even after raising a tornado of 5 kids. We love you and aspire to be more like you every day”.
