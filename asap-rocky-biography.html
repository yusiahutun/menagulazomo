Asap Rocky Bio (Wiki)

Publish date: 2024-05-26

Who is Asap Rocky?

Asap Rocky born Rakim Mayers is an American rapper, songwriter, record producer, and actor.

He is better known for his mixtape Live Love Asap (2011) and first debut album ‘Long. Live. Asap'(2013) which became an instant hit and tops the chart on the Billboard 200 in 2013.

Asap Rocky: Early Life, Parent, Nationality, Siblings, Education

Asap Rocky was born on October 3, 1988, in the Harlem neighborhood of New York City, US. His father, Adrian Mayers was from Barbados and died in 2012.

Rocky has an older brother, Ricky Mayers, and an older sister, Erica B. Mayers. Furthermore, he has a stepmother named Kari Mayers. There is no information regarding his schooling and college life.

He is of American nationality and Mixed (African-Barbadian-American) ethnicity.

Asap Rocky: Career, Profession

Asap Rocky started rapping from an early age of 9 after getting inspiration from his brother. Later his brother was shot dead and this tragedy made him more serious about rapping.

In 2007 Rocky joined a group of rappers based in Harlem known as ASAP Mob from which he adopted the nickname ASAP.

In 2011 he released two singles “Purple swag’ and ‘Peso’ which became the street anthem by then in New York.

Later in October, he released the mixtape single ‘Live Love Asap’ which became an instant hit and went on to sign a $3 million record deal with RCA Records and Polo Grounds Music.

After the success of Live Love Asap, he released his debut solo album Long Live Asap on January 13, 2013, which includes four hit singles four singles: “Goldie,” “Wild for the Night,” “Fashion Killa” and “F****n’ Problems”. this album tops Billboard 200 chart and sells 3 million copies in the US alone.

Furthermore, he released another studio album in 2014, At.Long.Last.A$AP and went on to give many instant hits like ‘Lead single’, “Lord Pretty Flacko Jodye 2, and finally, third studio album Testing has yet to be released.

Rocky’s net worth is assumed to be $10 million and his salary is unknown.
