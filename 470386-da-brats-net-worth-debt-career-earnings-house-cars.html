How Da Brat lost her money

On 31 October 2007, Da Brat was in an altercation where she argued with a hostess and attacked her from behind. She struck her in the face with a rum bottle, forcing the waitress to go to an area hospital. As a result, the police arrested and jailed Da Brat.

When she went to court, she pleaded guilty to aggravated assault, and the judge gave her a three-year prison sentence, seven years of probation, and 200 hours of community service. On 28 February 2011, the authorities released Da Brat from prison. Three years later, the victim she had assaulted in 2007 had started a civil trial.

The trial began on 24 February 2014 and ended on 28 February 2014. A jury awarded the victim $6.4 million for her injuries and past and future loss of earnings. But that was just the beginning. In August 2018, Da filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy. Since she owed many people a lot of money, filing for bankruptcy meant that her debtors and creditors would have to reorganise their payment plans.
