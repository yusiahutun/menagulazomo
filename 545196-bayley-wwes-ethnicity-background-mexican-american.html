Who is WWE’s Bailey?

Bayley is a professional female wrestler currently signed with WWE. She has been in the industry since 2008 and has been trained by Sara Del Rey, WWE Performance Centre, and Jason Styles.

Bayley, whose real name is Pamela Rose Martinez, was born in San Jose, California, on June 15, 1989. Her parents are Thomas and Annmarie Martinez. She followed in her parent’s footsteps and joined the wrestling profession.

Bayley’s father was a professional wrestler who wrestled in Independent Promotions on the West Coast under the name "Speedy". Her mother was a ticket salesperson and ring announcer for Big Time Wrestling, a promotion based in Hayward, California.

Bayley watched various wrestling competitions on TV when she was young and grew to admire performers such as Shawn Michaels, Eddie Guerrero, and Lita. She attended Independence High School in San Jose and once played as a captain of the basketball team. Bayley also took part in track and martial arts.
