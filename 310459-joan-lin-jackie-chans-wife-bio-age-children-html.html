How old is Joan Lin?

Joan Lin, born Joan Lin Feng-jiao, is 66 years old. She was born on June 30, 1953, in the province of Taipei, Taiwan. She was the second born out of a family of 5 children.

Joan Lin's early life

Joan Lin might be a successful actress, but she was not always rich. At the time of her birth, Taiwan was a haven for poverty. She was born into a poor family, and she (and her siblings) grew up without many of life’s comfort. Things were so bad for her family that she had to drop out of school at the age of 12 after her parents were unable to continue funding her education. However, events would later turn into her favour, when she got an acting role at the age of 19.

Joan Lin career

Joan Lin started her acting career at 19, just seven years after dropping out of school. Her first role was in the film, The Hero of Chiu Chow. The film was a huge success commercially, and this helped to open up more opportunities for her acting career. Within no time, her star flourished, and she became one of the household names in the Taiwanese entertainment industry. Her career lasted for 10 years, during which she featured in a total of 70 films. Some of the most memorable films she has starred in include: Shaolin Vengeance, My Native Land, The Story of A Small Town, the Battle for the Republic of China, Devil Returns, He Never Gives Up, and others.

After relocating to the US and marrying Jackie Chan, Lin left the entertainment industry. However, by then she had already made a name for herself, and even though she did not break out in Hollywood, she is still a star in Taiwan as several other countries where her films had a huge following.

Career achievements

In her short career in the entertainment industry, Joan Lin established herself as a star actress. However, her greatest achievement would not come until 1979, where she won the Best Leading Actress award in the 16th Golden Horse Awards for her role in the film, The Story of a Small Town. The previous year, Lin had been nominated for the same award for her role in the film, He Never Gives Up, but she did not win.
