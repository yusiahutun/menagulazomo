Actor Danny Trejo Brings His Taco Truck To White River Amphitheatre

Publish date: 2024-08-06

AUBURN, Washington (CelebrityAccess) — Actor Danny Trejo surprised fans at Rob Zombie & Marilyn Manson’s “Twins of Evil” tour stop at White River Amphitheatre in Auburn, Washington on August 3rd with Tacos.

Trejo dispensed tacos to hungry concertgoers, took selfies with fans at his taco truck and then went backstage to hang out with his longtime friend and co-star/director of the upcoming movie ‘3 From Hell,’ Rob Zombie.

Trejo has partnered with Live Nation to bring his signature Trejo’s Tacos truck to 21 venues across the country, including the White River Amphitheatre, as part of the promoter’s bid to raise the bar food offerings at its owned and operated venues.

Other partners and offerings on tap for 2019 as part of the program include: Questlove’s Cheesesteak with Impossible Burger meat, Art Bird’s fried chicken, Italian dishes from Top Chef’s Fabio Viviani and more.
