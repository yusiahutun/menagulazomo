Blake Lively channels Gossip Girl's Serena for stunning poolside 'Barbie' look

Publish date: 2024-03-02

Blake Lively channeled her inner Serena van der Woodsen for a stunning poolside look and many of the star’s fans called her a real-life Barbie.

The 35-year-old celebrity is not your typical star who likes to share everything going on in their personal and professional life on social media. However, when Blake posts a new snap on Instagram, she always makes sure to look spectacular and create huge engagement in her comments.

Blake Lively channels Serena van der Woodsen

The Hollywood star has turned into a real-life Serena van der Woodsen for a new snap on the gram. The actress played the iconic Gossip Girl character in all six seasons of the original TV series.

Blake rocked a two-piece orange swimsuit and covered her high-waisted bottoms with a white and yellow-striped towel.

She completed her stunning look, which many compared to a Barbie outfit, with heart-shaped glasses in a matching color, a couple of rings, and two low ponytails.

Fans compare Blake to Barbie

The actress received a lot of compliments for her stunning look as many drew similarities and Gossip Girl’s Serena and Barbie.

“Spotted…Serena van Derwoodson putting everyone on notice that she is and will always be the Queen…..sorry Barbie,” one fan reacted.

“Hi, Barbie,” another fan commented. “I’ve been rewatching Gossip Girl and I love your outfits you’re literally so stunning and the kindest character,” another fan of the TV show said.

Others speculated that the new image could be a teaser for a Taylor Swift video. One wrote: “Blake’s in her red era.”

A news outlet questioned: “Why is it giving Cruel Summer music video?”

Blake debuted a new line of drinks

Blake is a busy celebrity as she juggles several things at the time same. She began filming the 2024 romantic film It Ends with Us in May and develops her drink company Betty Buzz.

The actress launched a new line of sparkling cocktails called Betty Booze in addition to her non-alcoholic beverages.

The new drink is a sparkling lemonade cocktail which she got inspired to develop “from the lemonade recipe I made up 13 years ago for summer lemonade stands”, the star explained in an Instagram post.

Blake decided to debut the new drinks after her “loved ones wanted more” from her company and added that her team is developing a similar product without alcohol for Betty Buzz.
