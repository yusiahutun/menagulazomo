    When do the 4th of July sales start?

    Sales start a few days before the 4th of July, and you don't want to waste any time if you want to have the most options. As the calendar moves toward July 4, you may see bigger discounts at your favorite retailers – and some online stores even have amazing deals even past Independence Day – but as stock dwindles you might have fewer options to shop your faves.

    What stores have 4th of July sales?

    The good news is pretty much any major retailer will be offering up discounts, whether specifically for 4th of July or for the summer sales. My edit of faves includes my go-tos plus more great deals at Veronica Beard, Skims and more.
