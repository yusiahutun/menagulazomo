After Nicole Kidman's make-up fail, the top celebrity cosmetic blunders revealed

Publish date: 2024-02-22

Nicole Kidman is considered to be one of the most stylish, impeccably turned-out actresses in Hollywood.

But yesterday, the actress once again fell foul of a disastrous make-up job at the Women In Film 2015 Crystal + Lucy Awards in LA. 

She wore a glamorous black sequinned gown and had her hair teased into Fifties curls, but the look was ruined by her over zealous application of white face powder, which was plastered across her forehead, cheeks and nose. 

Nicole Kidman fell foul of a disastrous make-up job at the Women In Film 2015 Crystal + Lucy Awards in LA yesterday 

She had a similar cosmetics disaster at the 'Nine' film premiere in New York in 2009

It is the second time that the star has walked the red carpet covered in white powder - she was similarly caked in the cosmetic at the 'Nine' film premiere in New York in 2009. 

The 47 year old isn't the only star to have suffered at the hands of a make-up artist though, the likes of Angelina Jolie and Melissa Joan Hart have also been photographed sporting the white powder.

Women aren't the only victims either - even Jon Hamm had a sprinkling of the dust on his nose at a party in LA in 2012. 

Read on to see some of the worst celebrity make-up fails in recent times... 

ANGELINA JOLIE 

In May 2014, Angelina attended the New York premiere of HBO's new drama The Normal Heart with Brad Pitt caked in white powder

The actress displayed a powder disaster across her left cheekbone and forehead.

In May 2014, Angelina attended the New York premiere of HBO's new drama The Normal Heart caked in white powder. 

Despite looking stunning in a black gown for the event, the actress displayed a powder disaster across her left cheekbone and forehead. 

Celebrity makeup artist Jamie Greenberg told US Weekly at the time: 'What looks perfect in normal lighting can appear totally different when you have the harsh lights of flashing cameras on you.  

'Many women think you can just touch up with any powder for a red carpet event, but all powders have different finishing textures.'  

MELISSA JOAN HART  

Oops: Melissa Joan Hart sported a huge white smear across her forehead and chalky remnants around her eyes and nostrils at a film premiere in LA in November 2013

She became a household name as Sabrina The Teenage Witch, but magic couldn't save Melissa Joan Hart from an epic make-up fail at a film premiere in Los Angeles in November 2013.

The 38-year-old mother-of-three strolled confidently onto the red carpet seemingly unaware that her make-up artist had been more than a little heavy-handed with the translucent powder, leaving her with a huge white smear across her forehead and chalky remnants around her eyes and nostrils.

Once aware of the situation, the beaming smile quickly fell from the star's face and her once poised and confident posture was replaced with slumped shoulders and a look of defeat as she skulked off.

MILEY CYRUS  

Snow White: Miley Cyrus displayed white powder across her chin at the Maxim Hot 100 Party in May 2013 

From a distance, Miley Cyrus cut a striking figure in a sexy jumpsuit at the Maxim Hot 100 Party last May - but she was not ready for her close-up.

The 21-year-old had a visible layer of white powder around her chin after suffering a make-up malfunction while glamming up to celebrate her title as Maxim's hottest woman.

Loose face powder is meant to be used - sparingly - to minimise shine, but the application was a little heavy handed this time around.

KIM KARDASHIAN  

Even by her standards: Kim Kardashian, known for caking on the make-up, took it too far with thick base, overly long fake eyelashes and crimson lips at a Las Vegas event in 2013

She is hardly known for holding back when it comes to make-up.

But Kim Kardashian looked like a walking mannequin at a Las Vegas event in 2013.

The 33-year-old was overly enthusiastic for the outing, when she caked her face in base, and sported crimson lips and over-the-top fake eyelashes.

Whilst out and about for Paris Fashion Week Kim, who usually has perfect make-up, was spotted with a dodgy looking brown stripe going down her nose

Then in 2014, Kim showcased her dramatic new platinum 'do in Paris during Fashion Week, but had paid less attention to her make-up and stepped out with a brown line running down her nose which hadn't been blended properly.

Darkening the centre of the nose and highlighting the sides is a make-up artist's trick for making the feature look thin and straight.

The 34-year-old is well known for her love of highlighter and deep contouring which accentuates her cheekbones. 

LEIGHTON MEESTER  

Blair wouldn't approve! Leighton Meester wore garish purple eyeshadow made worse with red lips at a NYC event in 2009

She played a prim, perfectly groomed Upper East Side princess in the hit show Gossip Girl.

But Leighton Meester let down her on-screen alter ego with her garish make-up at an event in 2009.

The 28-year-old wore thick lashings of purple eyeshadow both above and below her eyes, black eyeliner and crimson lips, a look more suited to Halloween.

ASHLEY JUDD  

Time for a new make-up artist: She may be one of the most beautiful women in the world, but even Ashley Judd isn't immune from a bad make-up job, pictured in NYC in 2011

Her beauty is astonishing, but even Ashley Judd isn't immune to a bad make-up job.

The 46-year-old sported translucent powder under her eyes during a book signing in New York in 2011.

Her luscious short brown hair and rose-tinted lips were sadly not enough to detract from the powder disaster.

White powder under your nose is sure to raise a few eyebrows, but powder on your nose can also cause people to snicker, especially if you're a celebrity.

UMA THURMAN 

Blunder: Uma Thurman displayed white powder on her nose at an event in NYC in 2010

Uma Thurman fell victim to the powder effect at a gala in 2010.

The 44-year-old Pulp Ficton star sported a dusting of white on her nose and under her eyes, blissfully unaware as she smiled for the cameras.

AVRIL LAVIGNE  

Ghoulish: Avril Lavigne's eyes were barely visible under thick red fake eyelashes and glittery black eyeliner at her album launch party in 2007 in NYC

She may be a rock star, but Avril Lavigne took make-up experimentation to another level in 2007.

The 29-year-old sported ghoulish red fake eyelashes and a glittery cat-eye at her album release party in New York.

The eye make-up was so extreme that her blue eyes were barely visible under it all.

EVA LONGORIA  

Unaware: Eva Longoria displayed white powder under her eyes as she left a sushi restaurant in Hollywood with Kevin Connolly in 2011 

There's nothing 'desperate' about her, but even Eva Longoria can get it wrong in her quest to be beautiful.

The 39-year-old sported thick powder under her eyes after grabbing sushi with actor Kevin Connolly in Hollywood in 2011.

Luckily she wasn't pictured at an official event, saving her from heavy scrutiny. 

DREW BARRYMORE  

Ghostly: Drew Barrymore looked a fright at the 2003 London premiere of Charlie's Angels: Full Throttle

She famously got killed off in the first scene of the cult 1996 film Scream.

And Drew Barrymore certainly looked a fright at the 2003 London premiere of Charlie's Angels: Full Throttle.

The 39-year-old sported a white powder cast all around her eyes and cheeks as she posed for screaming fans outside The Odeon in Leicester Square.

JESSICA BIEL 

She usually looks flawless on the red carpet.

But Jessica Biel sported a touch of white powder on her nose at a film premiere in New York in 2010.

However, the faux pas did little to detract from her naturally gorgeous face.

Blink and you'll miss it: Jessica Biel sported a touch of white powder on her nose at a film premiere in New York in 2010

TYRA BANKS 

It's not Halloween! Tyra Banks looked like she had done her make-up in the dark with her thick black eyeshadow and neon pink lips at a ball in New York City in 2012

As a model, she gets to spend time with the world's top make-up artists.

But it seems Tyra Banks hasn't picked up many tips.

The 40-year-old looked like she had done her make-up in the dark with her thick black eyeshadow and neon pink lips at a ball in New York City in 2012.

JENNIFER ANISTON 

Not a wise choice: Jennifer Aniston tried to use make-up to cover-up the remnants of a cupping treatment, but the foundation only made the circles look worse at the Los Angeles screening of Call Me Crazy

It was a big night for Jennifer Aniston as she attended the premiere of a film she had directed.

But perhaps a strapless playsuit wasn't the best option for the actress due to the unsightly marks on her back.

The 45-year-old tried to cover up the round remnants of a recent cupping treatment, but instead the make-up only made the circles look worse as she arrived at the Los Angeles screening of Call Me Crazy in April 2013. 

JOHN HAMM 

It'snot just the ladies: Jon Hamm was caught with a dusting of white powder on his nose at a Paley Center benefit in Los Angeles in 2012

It's not just the ladies who have to worry about a bad make-up job. 

Jon Hamm was caught with a dusting of white powder on his nose at a Paley Center benefit in Los Angeles in 2012. 

The Mad Men star wasn't aware of the blunder as he gave his famous grin for the camera.
