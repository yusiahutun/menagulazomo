5 Cute Winter Mugs to Buy From Target

Publish date: 2024-06-14

Despite already longing for the warmer weather, there’s quite a bit to look forward to when it comes to fall and winter. For starters, we get to bundle up in cute sweaters and coats, cuddle up on the couch and watch seasonal rom-coms (without feeling guilty about wasting a beach day, at least), and sip hot beverages to fight off the chill.

Whether you prefer a steaming cup of tea, are a big coffee fan, or crave a classic hot chocolate is irrelevant. No matter which warm drink is your go-to, a fun mug turns an otherwise mundane activity into a full experience. Think about it: yes, you could sit at your desk or dining table, holding a simple solid cup. But you could also bring a little more joy into your life with a cute graphic or playful saying.

Thankfully, Target has plenty of winter mugs on its site right now. You could get super playful with a snowman shape that doubles as décor, or you can go kind of retro with a set that features stripes and skiers. No matter your vibe, we’ve rounded up a few of our favorites, ahead.

Wondershop Christmas Earthenware Snowman Mug White

There are plenty of cute winter mascots, but if you’re looking for a quirky mug that won’t feel out of place come January, this silly little snowman is ready to be added to cart.

Hearth & Hand with Magnolia 2pk 10oz Skier Figure Stoneware Mug Set

To some people, snow on the ground equals ski season. This colorful set is not only a nice nod to the cold weather activity, the simplistic design is super elevated and looks almost vintage.

If your teeth can’t stop chattering from below-freezing temperatures, pour something steamy into this mug and pretty soon, the text will become a reality.

Sweet Water Decor Homebody White and Gold Honeycomb Tile Coffee Mug – 17oz

Part of what we love most about the winter are those days when our main plan involves snuggling up under blankets and binge-watching movies. If you’re feeling like a homebody, too, make it even more official by sipping out of this metallic-accented mug.

Wondershop 16oz Christmas Stoneware Bite Me Mug White

If your mug preference is more funny and festive than simple and subtle, this partially eaten gingerbread cookie belongs in your collection. It would also make a great gift for someone who can be pretty grumpy before they’ve had caffeine.
