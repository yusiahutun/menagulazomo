Thai husband dies when he fell onto his angle grinder

Publish date: 2024-04-30

A husband was killed while fixing his house in Thailand when he dropped his angle grinder saw, which sliced through his neck.

Suwan Ladee, 54, was changing wooden beams infested with termites at his home in Samut Prakan province, Thailand, on February 28.

The father-of-one was standing on scaffolding and reached above him when he slipped and dropped his tool on his neck.

The angle grinder didn't have a protective guard on the blade and cut into his body.  

It sliced through his throat and chest spraying blood before he stumbled and fell to the ground in front of horrified friends helping him with the renovation.

Wife Nong, 52, said: 'My husband was working with four other people working all together to fix that house and get rid off the termites.
