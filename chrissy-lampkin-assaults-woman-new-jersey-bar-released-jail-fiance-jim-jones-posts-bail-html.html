Love And Hip Hop star Chrissy Lampkin 'assaults woman at New Jersey Bar and is released from jail after fiance Jim Jones posts her bail'

Love And Hip Hop star Chrissy Lampkin was arrested early on Sunday morning for allegedly beating a woman at a New Jersey bar.

The VH1 reality star was taken into custody around 2:30am for investigation of aggravated assault and disorderly conduct.

She was released a few hours later after her 37-year-old rapper fiance Jim Jones posted bail for her.

Under arrest: Chrissy Lampkin, shown in May in New York City, was arrested early on Sunday morning for allegedly assaulting a woman at a New Jersey bar

Officers with the Edgewater Police Department said the victim was bloodied in the alleged assault and required hospitalisation and stitches above her eye, according to TMZ.

Sources told the website the altercation started when Lampkin became angered after the alleged victim sat on the sofa she was sitting on and accidentally bumped into someone.

Lampkin rose to fame as a member of VH1's reality series Love And Hip Hop and appeared on the show's first two seasons following its debut in March 2011.

Hip hop couple: Chrissy and fiance Jim, shown in November on the Bethenny show in New York City, got engaged in late 2011

She and Jones got engaged in late 2011 after a lavish surprise party involving the whole cast of Love And Hip Hop.

They have their own reality spin-off show called Chrissy & Mr. Jones, which began airing in 2012 and concluded its second season earlier this month.

The altercation involving Lampkin apparently was not another case of reality show theatrics as the cameras were not rolling during the incident.

Outgoing personality: Chrissy, shown in June in New York City, recently finished the second season of her spin-off reality show Chrissy & Mr. Jones

Lampkin and her fiance Jones both grew up in the Harlem area of New York City.

Jones is an original member of the hip hop collective known as The Diplomats and also is the co-CEO of Diplomat Records.

Lampkin has become a stylist for Jones and also redesigned his recording studio.

Their VH1 reality spin-off show also show features Nancy 'Mama' Jones, who is the rapper's mother, and his 10-year-old son Joseph 'Pudie' Jones.
