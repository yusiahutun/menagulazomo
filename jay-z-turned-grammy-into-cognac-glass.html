Jay-Z Turned His Grammy Into a Cognac Glass

Publish date: 2024-06-19

Jay-Z was honored with the Dr. Dre Global Impact Award at Sunday night’s Grammys, and celebrated by casually turning his award into a cognac glass to drink from. I mean, might as well put this thing to use, right?

The rapper—who has numerous Grammy wins and nominations under his belt—gave a lengthy speech while accepting his award (with daughter Blue Ivy by his side!), calling out the Recording Academy for never giving his wife an Album of the Year award while he was at it.

“I’m just saying: We want [the Grammys] to get it right,” Jay said. “We love y’all, we love y’all, we love y’all—we want y’all to get it right, at least get it close to right. And obviously, it’s subjective. Obviously, it’s subjective, because, you know, it’s music and it’s opinion-based. But, you know, some things …”

He then gestured toward Beyoncé and continued: “I don’t want to embarrass this young lady, but she has more Grammys than everyone, and never won Album of the Year. So even by your own metrics, that doesn’t work. Think about that: The most Grammys, never won Album of the Year—that doesn’t work.”

“Some of you are gonna go home tonight and feel like you’ve been robbed,” he added. “Some of you may get robbed. Some of you don’t belong in the category. No, when I get nervous, I tell the truth.”

I mean, someone has to be real about this whole thing. Anyway, Jay ended his speech with an encouraging message to the audience: “Forget the Grammys, you got to keep showing up. Until they give you all those accolades you feel you deserve. Until they call you ‘chairman,’ until they call you a ‘genius,’ until they call you ‘the greatest of all time.’ You feel me? Thank you.”
