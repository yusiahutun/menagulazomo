Simon Kabu Ready to Pay to Access Sleek Phone after Forgetting Password: &quot;Sitaki Ikuwe Formatted&quot;

Publish date: 2024-06-22
  • Businessman Simon Kabu sought help from social media users after he could no longer access his sleek phone
  • The entrepreneur shared he did not want to flush his phone as he had over 5,000 photos he had taken during holidays
  • He said he was ready to pay anyone who would assist him and social media users flocked to the comment section to react to his post

Bonfire Adventures CEO Simon Kabu has turned to social media to seek technological help from Kenyans.

The successful businessman disclosed that he had recently bought a new high-end phone, a Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 5.

Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 5 specifications

The unique foldable phone costs over KSh 100,000 and has a long-lasting battery and powerful cameras which Kabu took advantage of.

"I need assistance, fam. I have this new phone—Samsung Fold 5—and I went with it for a holiday and took over 5,000 photos and videos. But I have forgotten the passcode (I can't use my fingerprint). I do not want it formatted, as I will lose all pictures. I am ready to pay to be assisted. Please reach me on +254713587664," he captioned.

Kabu shared a picture of the phone, whose primary colour looked gold.

He was stuck at the security page where he could only access the phone through his security password and not his fingerprint.
