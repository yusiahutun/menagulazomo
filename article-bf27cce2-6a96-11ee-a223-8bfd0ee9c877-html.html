Charles Buck Griffith | Obituaries

Publish date: 2024-02-16

Charles Buck “Brother” Griffith, 54 of Laurel, MS passed away Tuesday, October 10, 2023, at his residence in Laurel, MS. He was born Friday, May 2, 1969, in Laurel, Mississippi.

Visitation will be held on Friday, October 13, 2023, from 5:00PM until 8:00PM at Ellisville Funeral Home in 1204 B Ave, Ellisville, Mississippi. Funeral services will be held on Saturday, October 14, 2023, at 11:00AM at Ellisville Funeral Home in Ellisville, Mississippi. Burial will follow in Myrick Cemetery in Laurel, Mississippi. 

Bro. Robbie Johnson and Dr. Rory Dill will officiate. Ellisville Funeral Home is in charge of arrangements. 601-477-3797.

He was preceded in death by his father, Clyde “Buck” Griffith; niece, Anna Christine Manning.

Survivors include his mother, Daisy Clark Griffith; sister, Pam Manning.

Pallbearers will be Ben Yarber, Chance Yarber, Marcus Manning, Slade Dickerson, Matt Donald, Stan Yarber, Mark Manning, and Mark Griffith. 

An online guestbook may be signed at www.ellisvillefuneralhome.com
