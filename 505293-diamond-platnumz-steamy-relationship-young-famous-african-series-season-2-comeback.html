Diamond Platnumz to Get Steamy Relationship in Young, Famous &amp; African as Series Makes Season 2 Come

Publish date: 2024-08-09
  • Netflix has dropped a trailer announcing the official dates of the popular Young, African & Famous reality show
  • In the second season's trailer, Diamond Platnumz can be seen getting cosy with South African singer Nadia Nakai
  • Fans of the show have expressed how excited they are to keep up with the celebrities and are hoping the drama will be juicier

Netflix has announced the return of the popular Young, African and Famous show that debuted in 2022 casting top celebrities in Africa.

Diamond Platnumz brings drama to reality show

The show had Tanzania's singer Diamond Platnumz, his ex Zari Hassan, Nigeria's award-winning artiste 2 Face Idibia now known as 2Baba and his wife, designer Jerry Swanky among others.

In a trailer shared by Netflix on YouTube, the show featuring Africa's A-list celebrities is returning on May 19 and from the video, looks like it's going to be messier.
