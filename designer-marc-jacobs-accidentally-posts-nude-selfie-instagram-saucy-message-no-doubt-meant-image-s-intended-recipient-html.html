Marc Jacobs accidentally posts NUDE selfie on Instagram

Publish date: 2024-09-27

Designer Marc Jacobs accidentally shared a photo of his bare buttocks and penis with his 191,000 Instagram followers, reminding social media users to always look twice before hitting share.  

The 52-year-old presumably meant to send the sexy selfie via Instagram's direct message application on Monday night, but instead posted the candid snapshot for the world to see. Mr Jacobs quickly deleted the offending photo, but not before one fan snapped a screenshot and sent it to Gawker. 

'It's yours to try!' Mr Jacobs captioned the picture, which appears to have been taken in his closet.
