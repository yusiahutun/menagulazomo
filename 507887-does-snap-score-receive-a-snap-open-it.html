Does your Snap score go up when you receive a Snap or when you open it?

Publish date: 2024-01-29

Snapchat is one of the most popular social media platforms in the world. The app has a snap score feature that has caught the imagination of its users. Interestingly, there are several ways to increase your points. So, does your Snap score go up when you receive a Snap or when you open it?

Does your Snap score go up when you receive a Snap or when you open it? Several people on this popular social media app aspire to increase their Snap scores. Here is everything you need to know about it.

What is a Snapscore?

A snap chat score is a number assigned to a user's Snapchat account. It is determined by a special equation that combines the number of Snaps you’ve sent and received, the Stories you’ve posted, and a couple of other factors.

Snap score appears directly beside your username and can be seen by anyone. You can also have access to other users’ scores.

How does Snapchat score work? Snap score works by assigning a point to a user for every snap sent or received. You can increase your score when you send snaps, open snaps, and spend time watching stories.

How do you find your snap score?

Finding your snap score is pretty simple. Simply open Snapchat and tap on your profile icon in the top left corner. Additionally, when you visit your friends' Snapchat profiles, you can view their scores.

Does your Snap score go up when you receive a Snap or when you open it?

A higher Snapchat score means you send and receive photo and video snaps. Your score can increase without opening snaps in some cases. Here are some of the ways to increase your snap score;

1. Posting Snapchat stories

How does your snap score go up? Posting Snapchat stories is one way to increase your score. Every time you post your score, your score will increase. Your score will increase even more when your friends view and interact with your story.

2. Receive and send snaps

Does your Snap score go up when you receive a Snap or when you open it? Sending and receiving snaps is the primary and straightforward way to increase your score. It is essential to understand that you receive a point for sending and opening a snap.

3. Snapchat streaks

Starting a Snapchat streak with a friend will increase your score. Also, maintaining the streak by sending snaps every day will further increase your score.

4. Open Snapchat after some time

You can as well increase your score by opening the app. You do not need to necessarily send or receive snaps.

5. Participate in Snapchat challenges

Snapchat often runs challenges that users can participate in to earn points. Therefore, participating in these challenges is another way to increase your snap score.

6. Increase friends by adding new ones

What makes Snap score go up by 1? Every time you send or receive a Snap, your Snap Score increases by one point. However, adding friends on Snapchat will help increase your score. When you add a new friend, your score will increase. Also, when you send and receive snaps with them, your score will keep increasing.

7. View your friends' stories

When you view a story, your score will increase.

So, what does it mean when someone's Snap score goes up, but you aren't posting stories? If a user's score increases, it means that they have either sent or received more snaps. However, if a user is not posting stories, but the score keeps rising, it means they are using the app to send and receive snaps rather than post stories.

It could also mean that the user is not active in posting stories. However, he is sending and receiving snaps to and from friends.

Does your snap score increase with chats?

Unfortunately, no. Your Snapchat score is not determined by chats or messages. It is based on other activities you do on this social media platform. Therefore, if you wish to increase your score, the ways above would be useful.

And can someone's Snap score go up without them being active? Yes, it is possible to increase your Snapchat score without opening snaps. This can be done in a few ways, such as by sending out lots of snaps and interacting with other users on the platform.

How quickly does Snap score update?

Your snap score refreshes instantly after exchanging snaps. However, your friends can see your updated snap score after a few minutes or even hours. The same applies to you.

How beneficial is increasing your Snapchat scores?

No matter how high your score gets, it will not unlock any hidden or special feature. It will also not hand you any special privilege over users with lower scores. Below are some of the benefits of increasing your score.

  • It can be a way to track your own activity.
  • It can be a way to measure your engagement on the app.
  • It can be a way to prove your authenticity.
  • It can be a way to compare your activity to your friends.
