Chloe Bailey Is an Ethereal Goddess in a Shimmering Sheer Gold Gown

Publish date: 2024-06-26

Chloe Bailey looked like royalty Saturday night, as she waltzed across the purple carpet at the sixth annual Urban One Honors: Best in Black award show in Atlanta, shimmering and glittering in a gorgeous sheer gold gown.

Brian Stukes

The “How Does It Feel” singer is the latest star to take the see-through trend to the carpet—her iteration was dripping in shiny sequins. Bailey captivated in the floor-length, figure-hugging halter centerpiece by Retrofête, which featured an open back and was covered in mesmerizing rhinestones. She paired the dress with matching opera gloves, silver open-toe platform pumps, and diamond stud earrings. As for glam, she opted for a sultry smoky eye and nude lip, and styled her hair in hip-length braids.

At the ceremony, Bailey accepted the Generation Next Honor. The Urban One Honors celebrate “individuals whose remarkable achievements and significant contributions have left an indelible mark on the arts, media, music, and the community,” per the award show’s website.

Alongside Bailey, hitmaking singer Dionne Warwick was recognized as the Lifetime Achievement Honoree, soul and funk singer Frankie Beverly was honored as a Living Legend, songwriter and producer Donald Lawrence was honored for his Inspirational Impact, and Queen of Hip-Hop Soul Mary J. Blige was recognized as the Entertainment Icon Honoree.

NBC

Bailey has been spreading her sparkle in other shining ensembles. On January 18, she appeared on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon to perform BJ the Chicago Kid’s song “Honey” alongside him. For the occasion, she stunned in an earthy-brown jumpsuit adorned with gold, brown, and red beading all over. The textured centerpiece featured flared pants, a plunging zippered neckline, short sleeves, and a fitted bodice, which she further cinched with a wide brown suede belt. She finished off the look with glittery pointed-toe pumps, chunky gold hoops, and layers of chunky gold bangles.
