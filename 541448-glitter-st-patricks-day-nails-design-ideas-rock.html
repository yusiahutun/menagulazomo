27 glitter St. Patrick's Day nails design ideas to rock in 2024

St. Patrick's Day is celebrated on March 17 every year. When the day rolls around, everyone is looking for festive green wardrobes to rock on the occasion. But you need a themed manicure to match your wardrobe. If you are stuck on the choice of manicure design for your outfit, a list of glitter St. Patrick's Day nails designs should come in handy.

Saint Patrick's Day, also known as the Feast of Saint Patrick, is a religious and cultural holiday. It is a public holiday in the Republic of Ireland. The day commemorates Saint Patrick and the arrival of Christianity in Ireland. Celebrations generally involve public parades and festivals, with participants dressed in green attire or shamrocks.

1. Emerald gem green gradient nail design

Top on the list of nail art designs for St. Patrick's Day is the emerald game green gradient nail design. The emerald green nails idea will elevate your nail game to a whole new level of chic.

To achieve this look, gradually transition from light green to deep emerald. Then, add sparkling gemstones and glitter on each nail. The end result will be a luxurious look that will turn heads.

2. St. Patrick's Day tiny hearts nail design

If you are searching for a simple yet super cute heart nail art design that is perfect for your first St. Patrick's Day manicure, this design is for you. To achieve this look, paint tiny individual hearts onto the base of each nail. Then, create French tips and a larger heart on the other hand. The heart nail design will be love at first sight.
