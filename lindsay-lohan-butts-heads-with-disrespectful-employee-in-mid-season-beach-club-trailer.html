Lindsay Lohan Butts Heads with 'Disrespectful' Employee In Mid-Season 'Beach Club' Trailer

Publish date: 2024-06-13

The new hire, however, doesn't seem too impressed with her boss' IMDB credits.

There's some new blood coming to "Lindsay Lohan's Beach Club," but it looks like the boss and her new employee don't see eye to eye.

MTV just dropped an explosive sneak peek at the second half of the season on Monday morning, before tonight's new episode.

And while the footage starts off with glimpses of drag queens, bikini waxes and a whole lotta nudity, it quickly takes a serious turn. First, someone gets fired (our guess, based on who's MIA the rest of the clip, is Jules), before two new hires join the cast.

One, Kailah Casillas, came to the show fresh off of MTV's "The Challenge: Champs vs. Stars." Immediately, she and Lohan butt heads.

"Don't speak over me, that's disrespectful!" Lohan snips at her at one moment. In a confessional, Kailah then gets shady, saying, "I'm not going to let someone treat me bad just because they were in 'Mean Girls' one time, cool, I don't care."
