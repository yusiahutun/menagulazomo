Who is Sutton Stracke?

She is an American entertainer. She was born Sutton Thurman Brown in Columbia, South Carolina, on September 20, 1971. Her parents are John T Brown (father) and Reba Brown (mother). There are no details about her siblings.

She was raised in Augusta, Georgia, where her mother worked as a social worker for the Veterans Administration while her father was an architect in private practice. After her primary and high school education, she joined Converse University, a former women’s college in Spartanburg, South Carolina.
