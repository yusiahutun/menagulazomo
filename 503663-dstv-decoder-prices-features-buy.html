DStv decoder prices and features: Which one should you buy

Publish date: 2024-08-17

If you're in the market for a new DStv decoder, you may wonder how much they cost. Multichoice has better DStv decoders with improved signal reception and picture quality. DStv decoder prices depend on what you're looking for in terms of features and how much you're willing to spend.

Digital Satellite Television (DStv) has been one of Kenya and Africa's most popular broadcast service providers for nearly three decades. It was launched on 6 October 1995 and is owned by MultiChoice. It offers multiple channels and services to its broad customer base.

There are two types of DStv decoders, Zapper HD decoder and Explora. The availability of different DStv decoder types allows customers to select one depending on the experience they want and the number of Extra View connections they would want to make.

How much is DStv decoder at MultiChoice?

How much is a new DStv decoder? The price of the DStv decoder at MultiChoice ranges between Ksh. 999 and Ksh. 28,924. You can find more price information by dialling their USSD code, *423 #, on your mobile phone. These figures vary depending on the specific model and package being purchased. Below are the prices of the two brands of DStv decoders.

2. DStv Explora

The Explora is MultiChoice's flagship decoder, offering additional features such as recording live TV constantly and allowing you to pause and rewind. It also allows you to set recordings from the live TV guide. DStv Zapper HD decoder does not have these features.

Additionally, the Explora has a 2-terabyte hard drive, reportedly storing up to 220 hours of recorded content. Finally, it has a Catch Up Plus., which lets you catch up on missed episodes of your favourite shows.

  • DStv Explora full kit + installation Ksh. 35,899
  • DStv Explora + Smart LNB + Installation Ksh. 30,699
  • Explora Decoder + smart remote without installation Ksh. 28,924

The good news is that you can purchase decoders in the comfort of your home and at a discounted price if you order them using a USSD code, with free delivery. To do that, dial *423 # on your mobile phone. Select option 7, 'Buy a decoder', and follow the prompts.

A technician will be provided upon purchase at the cost of between Ksh 1500 and Ksh 2500 if you purchase without installation. It is highly recommended that only DStv installers carry out the installation. This is because the quality of your installation will determine your DStv experience.
