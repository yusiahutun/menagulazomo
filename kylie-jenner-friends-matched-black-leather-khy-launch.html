Kylie Jenner and Her Friends Looked Like the Coolest Biker Girl Squad

Publish date: 2024-04-26

What better way to celebrate the launch of your own fashion brand than by decking out all of your friends in clothes from the debut collection? That’s exactly what Kylie Jenner did last night at Santa Monica’s Giorgio Baldi, where she matched in black leather looks from Khy with sister Kendall Jenner and friends Hailey Bieber, Anastasia Karanikolaou, Yris Palmer, and Carter Gregory.

Kylie appeared in a sultry biker-chic look, composed of a zip-up leather halterneck top and matching leather trousers. She topped off the outfit with strappy black sandals, a small black bag, and black rectangular sunglasses.

Getty Images

Getty Images

Meanwhile, Kendall supported her younger sister in a skintight, strapless black leather midi dress, which she paired with sheer black Calzedonia tights and black pumps. The supermodel accessorized with a black clutch and small silver earrings.

Getty Images

Bieber wore a different strapless black leather dress, a minidress iteration featuring silver zippers all over. She layered the dress over a black bodycon mesh long-sleeved top and matching sheer Calzedonia tights, then capped off the look with narrow black shades from Otra, black pumps, a large black clutch, and a diamond anklet.

Getty Images

Kylie announced Khy, a high-fashion brand with a focus on accessible prices and sizes, on Wednesday.

“Khy is a homage to the limitless possibilities of fashion. We are breaking down barriers and will redefine the meaning of a designer brand, with creativity and quality at a better price,” she said in a press release. “I wanted to make Khy a platform for our customers to have the opportunity to experience fashion, through new designers and brands, but at a more accessible price.”

For Khy’s inaugural collection, Kylie partnered with Namilia founders Nan Li and Emilia Pfohl to create a 12-piece capsule of faux-leather pants, coats, tops, and basics, which will officially be available to shop on khy.com on November 1.
