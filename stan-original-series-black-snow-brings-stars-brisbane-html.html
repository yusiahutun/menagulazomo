Stan Original Series Black Snowbrings out the stars in Brisbane

Publish date: 2024-06-16

Some of Australia's most gifted and talented actors showed up for the Brisbane premiere of the Stan Original Series Black Snow on Friday.

Former Home and Away Lincoln Lewis led the pack of thespians at Event Cinemas in the Brisbane Myer Center. 

Other names in attendance included Offspring's Alexander England, ABC star Anthony J Sharpe, and Lantana's Daniela Farinacci.

Former Home and Away star Lincoln Lewis was one of the many names at the Brisbane premiere of the Stan Original Series Black Snow on Friday

The glamorous Jemmason Power, who stars in the crime drama, also turned heads in a stylish orange gown with a thigh-high split. 

Meanwhile, former Big Brother narrator Mike Goldman enjoyed the event by striking some animated poses on the blue carpet. 

In November, the streaming giant dropped the first trailer for the six-part series, starring Travis Fimmel.

ABC star Anthony J Sharpe and Lantana actress Daniela Farinacci posed together

Former Big Brother narrator Mike Goldman enjoyed the event as he posed for photos

The plot centres on a fictional 30-year-old unsolved murder that rocked a North Queensland South Sea Islander community. 

The whodunnit, which was produced in north Queensland, begins with the slaying of a teenager, Isabel Baker, in 1995. 

The killing rocked the small town of Ashford and devastated Isabel's community.

The glamorous Jemmason Power, who stars in the crime drama, also turned heads in a stylish orange gown with a thigh-high split

Logie Award winning Australian actor Rob Carlton was all smiles 

No one was ever caught for the murder, but in 2020 a time capsule unearths a vital clue that puts Detective James Cormack (Fimmel) on the path to solving the crime. 

Fimmel will star alongside Brooke Satchwell (Mr Inbetween), Alexander England (Alien: Covenant) and actor and comedian Rob Carlton.

Other cast members include Kym Gyngell, Erik Thomson and rapper Ziggy Ramo, who is making his screen debut in the series.

Alexander England and Joshua Macqueen are pictured

Australian singer Ziggy Ramo looked stylish on the blue carpet 

They are joined by newcomers Talijah Blackman-Corowa, Jemmason Power and Molly Fatnowna. 

The role of Detective Cormack will be a change of pace for Fimmel, a former Calvin Klein model who found fame on The History Channel series Vikings in 2013.

Fimmel is currently starring in the HBO Max sci-fi series Raised by Wolves.

The crime thriller Black Snow stars Travis Fimmel, who is best known for his stint on Vikings

The creative team for Black Snow includes creator Lucas Taylor (Harrow), Rosemary Blight (The Invisible Man), and co-producers Lois Randall and Kaylene Butler.

The show is produced by Sydney-based company Goalpost Pictures and is directed by Sian Davies (The Gloaming) and Matt Saville (Please Like Me).

The Stan Original series, Black Snow, premieres January 1 only on Stan.
