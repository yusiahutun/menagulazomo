12 chic scrunchies that will instantly elevate any look

Publish date: 2024-03-17

Good Squish

Good Squish

Designer Billie-Jo Cronin started Good Squish in 2021 in her living room, using her grandma's table linen. The brand has grown dramatically since then, and now has its own Hackney-based studio; it specialises in timeless pieces made out of deadstock fabrics.

The 'Blumberg' scrunchie in white is one of the brand's bestsellers and we can see why.

Rixo

As expected, Rixo's hair accessories are just as chic as the brand's much-loved vintage-inspired dresses. This particular style in a floral print will complement any ensemble, whether it's a cosy chunky knit and jeans in the winter, or a floaty dress come spring.

Saint Laurent

Saint Laurent

Saint Laurent's scrunchie is designed to give the illusion of a tied-up silk scarf, and will add instant glam to any outfit.

Playful yet sophisticated, this fluffy Jacquemus scrunchie makes a great statement.
