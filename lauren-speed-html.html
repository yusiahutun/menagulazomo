Lauren Speed-Hamilton - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-09-05

American reality star who is best known for having appeared on the first season of Netflix's popular reality series Love Is Blind. She is also known for having founded The Speed Brand, a popular creative multimedia company.

Before Fame

She first appeared on Netflix in 2020.

Trivia

She has worked professionally as a model and video producer. She has a popular Instagram with over 2.5 million followers. She is a self-proclaimed fitness fanatic.

Family Life

She got a dog in 2019 named Mr. Sparx. She married her Love is Blind match Cameron Hamilton in 2018. 

Associated With

She appeared on the Tamron Hall show in February 2020.
