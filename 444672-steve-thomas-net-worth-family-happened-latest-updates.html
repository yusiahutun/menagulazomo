Steve Thomas' biography

Steve was born in 1952 in Pomona, California, United States. He was raised in their hometown alongside five younger siblings. His passion for construction and renovation came from his father, who used to buy and repair old houses.

Thomas attended The Evergreen State College in Olympia, Washington. He graduated with a bachelor's degree in philosophy. He supported himself as a licensed painting contractor and carpenter while at the institution.

Steve was ambitious from a young age. At 13 years, he wanted to become a sailor, so he purchased his first sailboat. He became a carpenter in 1977. He worked on a 75-foot (23 m) ketch under construction in Antibes, France.
