*NSYNCs Joey Fatone To Host Common Knowledge Trivia Game Show

Publish date: 2024-02-05

Joey Fatone. Credit: People Magazine


LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – Game Show Network announced this week that former *NSYNC member Joey Fatone will be hosting their newest trivia game show, Common Knowledge. 

Common Knowledge will feature two teams of three players consisting of family, friends or co-workers answering everyday questions in an attempt to move to the next round to win $10,000.

The game show will not be Fatone’s first hosting gig. He previously served as an announcer on Family Feud from 2010 through 2015, and as host of The Singing Bee and Parents Just Don’t Understand.

The series is set to premiere on January 14 and will air Monday through Friday at 5:30 p.m. ET.
