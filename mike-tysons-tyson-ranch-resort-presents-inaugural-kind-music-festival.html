Mike Tysons, Tyson Ranch Resort Presents, Inaugural Kind Music Festival

Publish date: 2024-03-28


DESERT HOT SPRINGS, CA (CelebrityAccess) – Kind Music Festival (KMF) has announced the dates for its inaugural event.

Set to take place on February 23 in Desert Hot Springs, CA on the future site of Tyson Ranch Resort, KMF will have the distinction of being the first-ever all-ages music festival that embraces California’s updated cannabis regulations.

Created and owned by Mike Tyson, Tyson Ranch Resort is a 420-acre entertainment complex, luxury glamping resort and cannabis research & design facility, which is currently in development.

Tickets for Kind Music Festival go on sale to the general public Thursday, January 3 at 9:00 AM (PT) following the announcement of the festival line-up on Wednesday, January 2.

A portion of all proceeds from Kind Music Festival will be donated to non-profit organization, Standing United, which aids those struggling with drug addiction and homelessness, a cause that KMF holds dear as several employees, including a number of executives, are in recovery.

In accordance with 2019 laws, there will be no sales of cannabis on the festival grounds. For more information visit: www.kindmusicfestival.com.
