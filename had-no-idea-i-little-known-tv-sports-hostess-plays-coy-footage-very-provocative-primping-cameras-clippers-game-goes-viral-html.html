A relatively unknown TV sports personality who calls herself a 'sexy NFL hostess' has gained overnight fame thanks to her provocative primping that live cameras caught at an Clippers game Sunday.

Rebecca Grant's credits include modeling, acting as well as hosting, but she's almost instantly become better known for her performance as a breast-grabbing audience member at LA's Staples Center.

Grant has since coyly taken to Twitter, where she reposted the video while denying any knowledge there was a live camera two rows directly in front of her.
