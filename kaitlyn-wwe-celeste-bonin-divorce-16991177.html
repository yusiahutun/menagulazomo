Ex-WWE diva is single again after split with bodybuilder

Publish date: 2024-02-27

Kaitlyn, who held the title for five months in 2013, announced the split on her Instagram account.

Since leaving WWE in 2014, she's been running fitness the clothing company, Celestial Bodiez.

But now it seems her split with bodybuilder PJ Braun, who she married in 2014, has hit the business.

Taking to Instagram, Kaitlyn – who now goes by her real name Celeste Bonin – said PJ had hijacked her website.

She told her fans: "To address the rumours and everything that's flying around on the internet, PJ and I are splitting up.

"It has been my decision, we have been trying to keep it amicable, but today PJ decided to take ownership of the Celestial Bodiez domain."

Announcing a temporary move to a new website, celestialbodiezz.com, Ms Bonin apologised to her customers for the trouble.

"The new Celestial Bodiez team is working as hard as possible to keep business as usual," the 30-year-old added.

At time of writing, the original web address for Ms Bonin's business remains offline and cannot be loaded.

Mr Braun does not appear to have addressed the split with Ms Bonin on his social media accounts.

Daily Star Online has contacted Mr Braun for comment.
