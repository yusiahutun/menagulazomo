However, Chesney's still a fairly private person. There are plenty of things that even the No Shoes Nation — his most devoted superfans — might not know about the artist.

What's Chesney's guilty pleasure food? What's one of his bad habits? And why did he feel very, very fortunate after the Sept. 11, 2001, terrorist attacks? Keep reading to find out.
