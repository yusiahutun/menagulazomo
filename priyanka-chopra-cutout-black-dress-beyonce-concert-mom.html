Priyanka Chopra Wears a Cutout Black Dress to Beyoncs Concert

Publish date: 2024-02-10

Among the crowd at Beyoncé’s latest concert in London were Priyanka Chopra and her mom.

The Citadel star shared photos yesterday on Instagram of the fun outing.

In the first, Chopra is seen posing between her mother, Dr. Madhu Akhouri Chopra, and friend Tamanna Dutt. The actress wears a ruched black long-sleeved dress with a stomach cutout and a high leg slit on one side. It is adorned with colorful ribbons that match her colorful sandals. Chopra further accessorizes with a gold necklace and wears a bright red lip. Her hair is styled in a curly ’do.

The trio is seen posing in front of the stage at Tottenham Hotspur Stadium. Chopra’s mom and Dutt also wear all-black looks.

Other slides in the post include photos and videos of the performance, a close-up of Beyoncé’s dazzling golden Loewe bodysuit, a selfie of Chopra and friend Anisha Tee Gibbs, and an iconic photo of actress Salma Hayek kissing Chopra’s mom on the cheek.

“I dream it, I work hard. I grind ’til I own it, I twirl on them haters - Beyoncé,” Chopra wrote in the caption. “Damn! What a woman and what a night.”

She also thanked “Queen” Beyoncé and Jay-Z for “incredible hospitality” and husband Nick Jonas for treating her and her guests to the “most memorable” night. “Love u baby,” she wrote.
