Paws &amp; Claws Gala Returns

Publish date: 2024-05-04

The Treasure Coast’s largest shelter pet fundraising gala is back. This year’s Paws & Claws gala, benefiting the Humane Society of the Treasure Coast, is hotter than ever, embodying the theme “One Hot Havana Night—a sultry evening reminiscent of vintage Cuba.” The gala will take place March 4, from 6 to 11 p.m., at Hudson’s on the River.

The gala raises critical funds for animals in need and will benefit the shelter animals at the humane society. The annual event typically raises more than $225,000.

Guests will explore the venue while enjoying live entertainment by The Chase Band, as well as tasty passed bites, a raw bar and sushi station, and small plates to appeal to all palates.

Friends of the animals always have fun outbidding each other on popular packages up for grabs in the silent and live auctions. This year’s silent auction will go live February 24 for those who wish to get an early start or who cannot attend the gala. Tickets are $250 per person and may be purchased here.

For more information, contact Courtney Zanetti at (772) 600-3211 or CZanetti@hstc1.org.
