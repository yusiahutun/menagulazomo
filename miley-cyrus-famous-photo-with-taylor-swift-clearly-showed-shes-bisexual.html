Miley Cyrus' famous photo with Taylor Swift 'clearly showed she's bisexual'

Publish date: 2024-04-23

Miley Cyrus has finally found the “Be The Miley Of Your Group” meme and her reaction to the famous photo with Taylor Swift is as iconic as ever!

Miley has started a new TikTok series Used To Be Young where she looks back at some of her most iconic moments in pop culture discourse. From Miley’s songs which became an unexpected hit to the singer looking back at her childhood moments on TV with ‘creepy doll’ Willie Nelson, Miley is having a blast taking a walk down memory lane and she is bringing her followers along with her.

Miley Cyrus shares famous photo with Taylor Swift which ‘clearly showed’ she’s bisexual

Miley Cyrus is bringing back her old photo with Taylor Swift, Demi Lovato, and Emily which has become a popular meme, ‘Be the Miley Of Your group.” Miley quips looking at the picture and her dress, “If you guys didn’t know I am bisexual from this picture, then I don’t know what’s wrong with you.”

Miley is referring to a picture where they were leaving from the movie premiere for Hannah Montana: The Movie. Miley tried to recall the night of the picture gushing, “Me and Emily and Taylor Swift and Demi Lovato are going to the Cheesecake Factory. These are some classy ladies.”

Miley’s journey with sexuality after Disney days

Miley Cyrus reached the peak of her fame by starring in the Disney show Hanna Montana. The series went on for four seasons from 2006 to 2011. It took Miley a couple of years after that to talk about her sexuality openly. In 2015, the Plastic Hearts singer came out as pansexual.

“My whole life, I didn’t understand my own gender and my own sexuality. I always hated the word ‘bisexual,’ because that’s even putting me in a box. I don’t ever think about someone being a boy or someone being a girl,” Miley said that time in an interview.

“Used to Be Young; celebrating who we will become”

Miley Cyrus has been sharing her life through the TikTok series “Used To Be Young” which is also a single of the pop queen who shared that the song holds a special place in her heart. Miley shared while releasing the song, “This song is about honoring who we’ve been, loving who we are, and celebrating who we will become. I feel proud when reflecting on my past and optimistic when thinking about the future.”
