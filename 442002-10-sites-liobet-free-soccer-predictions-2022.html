10 sites like Liobet to get free soccer predictions in 2022

Publish date: 2024-04-02

Liobet is a website that offers free soccer predictions, betting tips, and picks. The site helps users find value bets they can use to profit long-term. A unique factor about Liobet is that it does not gamble on the tips it gives its users. Instead, it only picks games and betting markets with a high chance of occurring. That is one of the reasons you will only get bets with small odds compared to other sites like Liobet that offer high odds and accumulators to maximise profits.

Many punters spend their time looking for sports betting tips, guides, or strategies for online betting. One idea you may have encountered is that low odds are better for making profits in the long run. Essentially, low odds are bets with odds of not more than 1.60.

The primary reason for choosing small odds is to make safe bets. However, that also means you will have to be patient to make money in the long run. Liobet specialises in low odds, and its success rate has been high compared to other tipping websites.

Is it worth it to bet on small odds?

Betting on small odds is a safe way of securing profits by minimising risks. When you bet on low odds, your chances of winning the bet are high. However, the returns are small because you only choose the favourites to win. But no betting strategy is foolproof. Therefore, only stake an amount of money you can afford to lose.

Best sites like Liobet for free soccer predictions

Getting free soccer predictions for today is easier because of the many online platforms that help punters make money betting on sports. Some websites enjoy a high accuracy, while others require you to research further to get the best bets. But if you want a site that offers the best odds, whether low or high, here are alternatives to Liobet that are worth considering.

1. ProSoccer.gr

ProSoccer.gr has been a reliable online resource for getting free football betting tips. The platform offers the probabilities of each football match, allowing you to determine the chances of a team winning the game. But the website has been the best for finding bets on other markets, including Correct Score and Over/Under 2.5.

ProSoccer.gr also displays the average odds from bookmakers. The bookies’ average odds make it easier for you to compare odds and determine a team’s chances of winning a match.

2. Vitibet

Vitibet provides football betting tips, predictions, and analyses. The platform has some of the most accurate tips on the internet. However, it uses algorithms and only displays a prediction based on the last six league games.

In other words, the teams facing each other must have played at least three home and away games before calculating their chances of winning. Vitibet covers the 1X2 betting market. However, it is best for users that want low odds to bet on Double Chance.

3. Betensured

Betensured is a website that offers free and premium betting tips and predictions. Like Liobet prediction, Betensured is one of the best websites to look for bets with small odds. The platform covers many betting markets, including 1X2, Double Chance, Both Teams to Score, Over/Under 2.5, and Draw No Bet.

However, the Double Chance market on Betensured has a high accuracy compared to other tips provided on the platform. Also, punters looking for Over/Under 3.5 tips with small odds can use the site to get the best bets.

4. VictorsPredict

VictorsPredict is an online service that provides free football predictions and tips. It is also the best platform for free football analysis, the latest results, and league tables. The website is an excellent Liobet alternative because it offers tips with a higher chance of occurring.

For example, VictorsPredict has the Double Chance tips with small odds but give you a higher than 33% chance of occurring. You can also get other tips, including Both Teams to Score, 1X2, Over/Under 2.5, and Draw No Bet.

5. Typersi

Typersi is an online betting tipster competition that gives users free betting tips and predictions. Tipsters with excellent knowledge of football betting offer their picks for the day, allowing other punters to copy and use them to make informed bets.

Tipsters on Typersi only offer minimal odds of 1.50. Also, you can get tips on many markets with the highest winning probability. Apart from football, Typersi also offers betting tips on basketball and tennis.

6. TelecomAsia

TelecomAsia provides free sports betting tips and predictions. It is also a helpful online resource for getting football reviews, the latest sports news, and insights. In addition, you can use it to find the best betting offers and bookmaker reviews. An advantage of the site is that it analyses upcoming matches before giving users its predictions.

It considers crucial information between two teams, including past records, H2H data, and previous results. That helps you to get informed on the bet before wagering. In addition, TelecomAsia covers all the traditional football betting markets.

7. Free Super Tips

Free Super Tips offers free betting tips on all known sports. It has a team of experienced tipsters who analyse upcoming games and use their knowledge to provide winning tips, picks, and predictions. The website is helpful for punters that prefer accumulator bets with high winning percentages.

Free Super Tips covers many football betting markets, including 1X2, Both Teams to Score, Anytime Goalscorer, Correct Score, and Over/Under 2.5. However, it enjoys high accuracy on the Over/Under 2.5 market. Thus, it is best for punters that want to minimise the risk by opting for Over/Under 1.5 or 3.5.

8. Feedinco

Feednico is a website focused on predicting sporting events. It offers daily previews, analysis, and betting reviews of major leagues worldwide. You can also get betting tips and predictions for tomorrow and the entire week.

Feednico uses algorithms to offer its betting predictions. Before displaying its predictions, it first gathers team data from the last six games, three home and away games. As a result, the tips on the page have higher accuracy and come with value odds.

9. Supatips

Supatips is a football prediction website like Liobet. It predicts football matches across the globe but only displays high-value bets. The free tips are the best for punters that prefer low odds.

Supatips enjoys a high accuracy on the Double Chance football betting market. Also, you can get value bets on other small markets, including Over/Under 1.5, Draw No Bet, Over 0.5 Half-Time.

10. Affirmbetz

Affirmbetz is an excellent alternative to Liobet. It is best for punters looking for long-term profits by wagering games with small odds. The website offers tips on matches with a more than 33% chance of occurring.

You can get free picks and tips on Double Chance, Over/Under 1.5, Draw No Bet, and Both Teams to Score. Although the odds are low, their high accuracy means you can combine them in an accumulator to win more money.

The advantage of using Liobet is that the site gives you free bets with a higher chance of winning. However, the odds for those accompanying bets are low. But if you want to maximise your profits, you can consider other sites like Liobet that provide higher odds. The best alternatives to Liobet are ProSoccer.gr, Vitibet, Betensured, and Affirmbetz.
