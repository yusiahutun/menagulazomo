A former HBO executive producer has been hauled before the family court again as the mother of one of his children fights to get higher child support payments from him.

Rachel Rosen is the third woman to have had a child by Michael Fuchs. A fling with a Playboy photographer 21 years ago gave him his first daughter and then 14 years ago a Connecticut woman gave him a son.

The socialite gave birth to his daughter seven months ago and at the time, Fuchs denied being the father. A later court-ordered DNA test proved paternity.

All three mothers had to sue him for paternity.

Princess Yasmin Aga Khan with Michael Fuchs pictured in 2001: The former HBO executive is claiming he only gets paid $180,000 a year and can't pay any more in child support

The 67-year-old was back in Manhattan Family Court yesterday after Rachel Rosen asked that he up the $3,500-per-month he pays for the care of their boy Henry as well as paying for his education.

Her attorney Eddie Hayes told the New York Post: 'He pays tuition for his other children. If he was a gangster and he was this greedy, they’d kill him as a warning to other deadbeat dads.'

But the executive is claiming he only makes $180,000 a year. Rosen estimates his worth to be $30million.
