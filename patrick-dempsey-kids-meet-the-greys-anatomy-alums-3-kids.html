Patrick Dempsey Kids: Meet the 'Grey's Anatomy' Alum's Children

Publish date: 2024-08-20

Patrick Dempsey may be known as Dr. Derek “McDreamy” Shepherd to fans on Grey’s Anatomy, but he plays a much bigger role off-screen: He’s the loving father of three wonderful kids. After tying the knot with wife Jillian Fink in 1999, the iconic actor went on to become the proud dad of Talula, Sullivan and Darby.

Patrick and the Crew actress started their family when they welcomed their eldest child, daughter Talula, in 2002. The Made of Honor star and Jillian seemingly relished in their new lives as a dad and mom as they waited nearly four years before expanding their family. In 2007, the couple completed their clan when their twin sons, Darby and Sullivan, arrived.
