Snowball Festival Announces Lineup Additions

Publish date: 2024-04-11

DENVER, Colorado (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) – The SnowBall Music Festival has announced the latest additions to the lineup for their festival, which is set to take place at Mile High at Sports Authority Field on April 4, 5, and 6.

Coinciding with the on-sale date of single-day tickets, the festival announced that they have added Michal Menert and Earl Sweatshirt to the lineup which already includes Pretty Lights, Knife Party, Mimosa, Busta Rhymes, Kill The Noise, and Yeasayer among numerous others.

Also included at this year's festival will be a one-of-a-kind PLM Party curated by headliner Pretty Lights and featuring PLM family and friends Michal Menert, Eliot Lipp, Paul Basic, and SuperVision as well as other guests TBA.

Snowball is alsl hosting their opening DJ competition, which launched on Feb. 20th. The competition allows up-and-coming DJ talent the chance to compete for two opening DJ slots at the festival with the winners decided by popular vote.

Discounted GA and VIP 3-Day Passes, as well as Single day are on sale. For more information, check them out online at snowballmusicfestival.com. – Staff Writers
