Tom Holland adores Zendaya's new hair and fans can't cope - 'This was made for me'

Publish date: 2024-01-30

Tom Holland remains Zendaya’s biggest fangirl, and like us, he’s digging the new hair. Of course, he had to share his love for his partner for the world to see, and we can’t cope.

The celebrity couple confirmed their relationship two and a half years ago, after meeting on the set of Spider-Man: Homecoming in 2016. Although there were fears of a breakup between the two, Tom has set the record straight that he’s all for Zendaya.

Tom Holland reacts to Zendaya’s new hair

This week, the Euphoria star showed off a new hairstyle at Paris Fashion Week, some daring choppy bangs, with sleek straight hair.

Although Tom Holland wasn’t by his partner’s side at the Schiaparelli runway show, he’s shown his support from afar.

Taking to his Instagram stories, the 27-year-old posted a black and white photo of Zendaya stunning in her Schiaparelli look. The all-black look featured a black turtleneck with spikes running down the sleeves. The eccentrics didn’t stop there though, with a black skirt and super long train, that needed some assistance holding up!

Showing his love, Holland simply captioned the picture with three love heart eye emojis, but that wasn’t all.

He then reposted a reel to his story, by Photographer Bilal Zagaoui, who got some slay shots of the 27-year-old. In the background, the song Cupid’s Chokehold could be heard, along with the lyrics: “Take a look at my girlfriend, ’cause she’s the only one I got.”

Holland then wrote: “This was made for me”. Yes, it certainly was Tom.

Fans are ‘fangirling’ harder than Tom

If there’s anyone who stans Tom’s relationship more than himself, it’s the fans. Of course, they grouped on to X, formerly known as Twitter, to celebrate this moment together.

“Tom Holland is so real for thirsting on a random Tuesday evening,” wrote one.

A third gushed: “These two really hit the jackpot with each other.”

“Knowing they continue to be a couple reassures me. It’s heartening to witness their ongoing connection,” tweeted a fourth.

One joked: “He really NEVER coming up off her I love that for them LMAO.”

Couple goals!
