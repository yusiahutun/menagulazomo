8 of Faith Hill's Sexiest Instagram Moments

Publish date: 2024-03-24

Faith Hill has wowed country fans with her aura for decades, but when she posted her first Instagram photo in 2012, it launched a new social era for the talented female.

Hill is one of country music's most beloved stars and her husband, fellow country icon Tim McGraw, have come together for some saucy photos since the beginning of their marriage in 1996. Some of those photos are spicing up Hill's Instagram page as we speak!

McGraw always gets attention for being one of country's sexiest acts, but the man would not be the man without the woman, the mother of his three children. Shoutout to her for doing it her way since she made her way onto the scene.

1. The way he loves her 20 years later.

Hill posted a photo of the two on McGraw's birthday, having a private moment (well, besides the photographer) in the water. Saucy!

2. Rocking the stage like only Faith can.

Hill, seen here rocking a tight black dress, still has the ultimate amount of grace onstage. As part of the Soul2Soul Tour with hubby McGraw, she showed she's still on top of her game.

3. Ice cream, anyone? (Yes, please).

If Steak 'n Shake made this a permanent ad, we bet they could sell a lot of milkshakes.

4. She's 50 and fabulous — Faith has still got it.

Still extremely elegant, Hill shows off her casual side in this photoshoot from 2013.

5. Lucky dog!

What's not sexy about someone who loves animals? Here, Hill is seen with the couple's Italian Bracco, Palio. According to the post, Palio knows how to "speak to a girl."

6. A woman and her guitar ... 

A stunning shot of Hill under the lights, feeling the music with guitar in hand.

7. Is this too hot for Instagram?

Hill and McGraw sure know how to set these black and white photos on fire. Even with their faces mostly covered, this pic screams steam.

8. Below the belt — imagine what happens when cameras are off!

This Valentine's Day post is all about love and commitment. Hill and McGraw aren't exactly holding hands, but with where their hands are placed, we think they're still into each other quite a bit.
