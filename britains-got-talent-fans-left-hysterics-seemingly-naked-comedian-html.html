Britain's Got Talent fans left in hysterics over seemingly 'naked' comedian

Publish date: 2024-08-19

Britain's Got Talent judges got more than they bargained for on Saturday night when comedy act Tonikaku (Toni) showed off a series of seemingly naked poses. 

And fans at home were left in hysterics over the comedian's act, claiming it was 'the funniest thing' they'd seen all year and called for him to be 'put in front of the king.'

Judges Simon Cowell, Alesha Dixon, Amanda Holden and newcomer Bruno Tonioli, screamed with laughter as Toni rolled around on stage and posed. 

Donning a tiny pair of flowery pants, Toni demonstrated how he could pose 'naked' while keeping his dignity somewhat protected by his underwear. 

Sharing their thoughts on Twitter, one viewers penned: 'Toni on #BGT was absolutely brilliant, I was laughing start to end..Brilliant Act.'

The BGT judges and audience were initially confused when Toni, who donned a pair of peach and white pants, announced he was to showcase a selection of 'naked' poses.

After revealing he was at first going to demonstrate a 'football player naked pose', everyone watched on tenterhooks as Toni pretended to dribble an invisible football while funky music played in the background.

He then pretended to kick the ball and positioned himself so that his leg completely obscured his pants, giving the illusion of him being naked.

As everyone roared with laughter at the pose, Toni was then quick to reassure everyone he was keeping things PG, remarking: 'Don't worry, I'm wearing....pants!'

His second pose was that of a 'naked horse racer', with Toni mimicking racing movements before bending over like he was mounting a horse, with his belly then hiding his pants.

At this, Alesha, Amanda and Bruno jumped out of their seats as they laughed at Toni's act, with the funnyman quick to remind everyone that he was indeed 'wearing pants!'

The third pose saw the comedy star channel his inner James Bond as he rolled across the floor before finishing in the spy's classic gun pointing pose - with Toni's colourful pants once again hidden from view.

His quirky performance certainly went down well with everyone, with Ant McPartlin quipping: 'I love it so much!' while Alesha branded it 'genius!'
