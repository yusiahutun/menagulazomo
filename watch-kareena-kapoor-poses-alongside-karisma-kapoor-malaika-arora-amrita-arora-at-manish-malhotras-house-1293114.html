WATCH: Kareena Kapoor poses alongside Karisma Kapoor, Malaika Arora, Amrita Arora at Manish Malhotra

Publish date: 2024-02-22

Kareena Kapoor Khan is currently enjoying the success of her film Crew, which also features Kriti Sanon, Tabu, and Diljit Dosanjh in key roles. The actress recently attended a dinner party at Manish Malhotra's house in the city. She was accompanied by her sister Karisma Kapoor and best friends Malaika Arora and Amrita Arora. All the ladies looked stunning, dressed to impress for the occasion.

Kareena Kapoor attends a dinner party with her girl gang

Over the weekend, Kareena Kapoor Khan and her fabulous girl squad painted the town red as they glammed up for a dinner soirée at Manish Malhotra's lavish abode. The dazzling divas, including sister Karisma Kapoor and BFFs Malaika Arora and Amrita Arora, turned heads with their impeccable style and infectious energy. Kareena Kapoor Khan and Karisma Kapoor's work front

Last year Kareena made her OTT debut with the mystery thriller film Jaane Jaan alongside Jaideep Ahlawat and Vijay Varma. She also acted and co-produced The Buckingham Murders which is slated to release soon. A couple of days ago, her heist comedy film Crew was released.

The movie garnered decent reviews and also stars Tabu, Kriti Sanon, Diljit Dosanjh, and Kapil Sharma. As of now, she is busy filming for her upcoming action film Singham Again. Directed by Rohit Shetty, it also stars Ajay Devgn, Akshay Kumar, Ranveer Singh, Deepika Padukone, Tiger Shroff, Arjun Kapoor and Jackie Shroff.

Meanwhile, Karisma was last seen in Netflix's Murder Mubarak. The film also features Sara Ali Khan, Vijay Varma, Tisca Chopra, Sanjay Kapoor, Dimple Kapadia, and Pankaj Tripathi.
