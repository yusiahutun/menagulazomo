Helen Flanagan wears slippers wrong in Instagram post

Publish date: 2024-08-20

She is known for playing the ditsy Rosie Webster on beloved ITV soap Coronation Street. 

And Helen Flanagan appeared to accidentally channel her scatterbrained character on Friday, as she suffered a fashion mishap in her latest Instagram snap.

The actress, 26, took to the social media site to show off her new pair of slippers, but embarrassingly wore them on the wrong feet - a blunder which did not go unnoticed by her eagle-eyed followers.

'Then I thought, I’m not doing anything wrong – just because I’m going to work doesn’t mean that I’m a bad mum. I cried the first day I dropped her off at nursery.'

Since giving birth to Matilda in June 2015, Helen claims she has never been happier and is relishing adding to her brood.

‘I am a million times happier,' said the star. 'I love being a mum. I’ve always wanted to be a mum. Matilda is my pride and joy. She’s my everything. It has given me a meaning and purpose in life.

'We definitely want more – I think we want to wait until Matilda is four years old. We love her more than anything, but she is definitely enough for us at the moment.’
